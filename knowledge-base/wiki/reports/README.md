# Applied Value Report Sections

This directory contains the 9 standard sections of the Applied Value semi-annual Building Materials & Building Products industry report. Each section maps to a category of intelligence gathered by the KB system.

## Sections

1. **Intro & Executive Summary** — High-level overview and key takeaways
2. **Market Scope** — Market definition and boundaries
3. **Market Context & Outlook** — Current conditions and forward-looking analysis
4. **Drivers of Market Health** — The 7 key drivers (rates, labor, materials, demand, infrastructure, credit, GDP)
5. **Public Company Performance** — Financial analysis of 35 tracked companies
6. **Positioning for EOY** — Strategic positioning and year-end outlook
7. **Trend Continuity & Retrospective** — Ongoing trend assessment and prediction look-back
8. **How AV Can Help** — Service offerings relevant to current conditions
9. **Public Company Performance Snapshot** — Condensed metrics and comparison tables

## Usage

Articles in the KB are automatically tagged with relevant report sections by the `scripts/tag-articles-with-av-sections.ts` script. This enables:
- Filtering articles by which report section they inform
- Tracking coverage density per section
- Identifying gaps in intelligence for the next report

# Installed Building Products Inc.

## Company Overview
Installed Building Products is the largest U.S. installer of insulation and complementary building products. The company pursues an aggressive bolt-on acquisition strategy focused on geographic expansion and service capability enhancement. IBP reported record profitability in Q4 and FY2025 despite broader sector weakness.

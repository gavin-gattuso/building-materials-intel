# Lowe's Companies Inc.

## Company Overview
Lowe's is the second-largest home improvement retailer in the U.S. and is aggressively pursuing the professional contractor (Pro) market through major acquisitions. The $8.8 billion Foundation Building Materials deal and Artisan Design Group acquisition represent a strategic transformation toward "Total Home" service for large Pro customers.

### FBM Integration Details
- Over 370 locations across US and Canada
- Leading distributor of interior building products (drywall, metal framing, ceiling systems, doors, insulation)
- $250 billion total addressable market for professional customers
- Cross-selling opportunities with Artisan Design Group

## Market Context
- Housing market described as "frozen" by management
- Home improvement demand remains tepid due to mortgage rate lock-in effect
- Conservative guidance reflects ongoing macroeconomic uncertainty
- Competing directly with Home Depot for pro channel dominance

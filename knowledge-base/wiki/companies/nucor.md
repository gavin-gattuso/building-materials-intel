# Nucor Corporation (NUE)

## Company Overview

Nucor Corporation is the largest steel producer in the United States and the largest steel recycler in North America, headquartered in Charlotte, North Carolina. The company pioneered the use of electric arc furnace (EAF) technology for steelmaking, which uses recycled scrap steel as primary input, making Nucor the most sustainable major steel producer in the world. Nucor operates through three segments: Steel Mills, Steel Products, and Raw Materials.

Nucor operates approximately 30 steel mills across the United States, producing a full range of steel products including sheet, plate, structural, bar, rebar, and hollow structural sections. The Steel Products segment manufactures steel joists, girders, deck, buildings, cold-finished steel, fasteners, steel grating, and wire products. The Raw Materials segment includes DRI (direct reduced iron) facilities, scrap brokerage, and natural gas operations.

With 2025 revenue of $32.49 billion and over 32,000 employees, Nucor is the dominant US steel producer. The company's growth strategy combines substantial organic capital investments (including a new $4 billion sheet mill in West Virginia) with disciplined M&A. Nucor achieved record-low injury and illness rates for the eighth consecutive year in 2025, reflecting its strong safety culture.

## Tariff & Trade Exposure

- **Major Section 232 Beneficiary:** As the largest US steel producer, Nucor is a primary beneficiary of 50% steel tariffs
- **Domestic Production:** Virtually all production in the United States, fully insulated from import duties
- **Scrap-Based Production:** EAF technology uses domestic scrap, limiting raw material tariff exposure
- **Competitive Advantage:** Tariffs reduce import competition, supporting domestic pricing and utilization rates
- **Advocacy:** Nucor has been a strong advocate for maintaining and expanding Section 232 tariffs
- **Derivative Products:** Expansion of tariffs to steel derivative products further benefits Nucor's downstream Steel Products segment
- **Net Assessment:** Strongly net positive from tariff environment

# Advanced Drainage Systems (WMS)

## Company Overview
Advanced Drainage Systems (ADS) is the largest manufacturer of high-performance thermoplastic corrugated pipe and related water management products in North America. Headquartered in Hilliard, Ohio, the company provides a comprehensive suite of stormwater and onsite wastewater management solutions for residential, non-residential, agricultural, and infrastructure end markets.

ADS has been transforming from a pipe manufacturing company into a full-scale water management solutions provider. This evolution was dramatically accelerated by the $1.0B acquisition of National Diversified Sales (NDS) in early 2026, which added landscape irrigation, residential water management, and access box products to the portfolio. Combined with its existing stormwater drainage, retention/detention, and filtration systems, ADS now offers one of the broadest water management product portfolios in North America.

The company benefits from several secular tailwinds: aging water infrastructure, increasingly stringent stormwater regulations, the shift from concrete to thermoplastic pipe (lighter, cheaper, easier to install), and growing emphasis on sustainable water management in both residential and commercial construction.

## Recent Earnings

### FY2026 YTD Performance
Through the first nine months of FY2026, ADS has demonstrated consistent margin improvement with adjusted EBITDA margins exceeding 30%, driven by favorable pricing, recycled material cost advantages, and operational leverage.

## Strategic Initiatives

- **Water Management Platform:** Evolving from pipe manufacturer to full-scale water management solutions provider spanning stormwater, onsite wastewater, and now landscape irrigation.
- **NDS Integration:** Near-term priority to capture cost synergies and cross-sell opportunities from the NDS acquisition.
- **Recycled Materials Advantage:** ADS uses recycled HDPE (high-density polyethylene) as a primary raw material, providing both cost and sustainability advantages over virgin material competitors.
- **Infrastructure Spending:** Positioned to benefit from federal infrastructure investment in water management systems.
- **Market Share Gains:** Continuing to take share from concrete and metal pipe alternatives through product performance and installation cost advantages.

## Tariff & Trade Exposure

ADS has relatively low direct tariff exposure given its predominantly North American manufacturing footprint and domestic raw material sourcing (recycled HDPE from U.S. sources). However, tariffs on imported resin, equipment, and specialty components could modestly increase costs. Rising material costs (particularly recycled polypropylene and HDPE) represent a more meaningful input cost risk than tariffs.

# Fortune Brands Innovations (FBIN)

## Company Overview
Fortune Brands Innovations is a home and security products company operating through three segments: Water Innovations (Moen faucets and fixtures), Outdoors & Security (Therma-Tru doors, Fiberon decking, Master Lock, SentrySafe), and Connected Products (Yale and August smart locks, digital security). Headquartered in Deerfield, Illinois, the company was formed from the 2022 transformation of Fortune Brands Home & Security into a digitally-focused innovator.

Fortune Brands holds the number one or two market share position in most of its core categories, with Moen being the top-selling faucet brand in North America. The company has strategically pivoted toward connected and smart home products, highlighted by the $800M acquisition of Assa Abloy's residential business (Yale and August smart locks) in 2023, which created the Connected Products segment.

With approximately $4.5B in annual revenue, Fortune Brands is positioned at the intersection of plumbing, security, and smart home technology. The company generates the majority of its revenue from repair and remodel activity, providing some insulation from new construction cyclicality.

## Strategic Initiatives

- **Digital & Connected Transformation:** Building a connected home ecosystem through Yale/August smart locks, digital water products (Moen Smart Water), and integrated security solutions.
- **China Restructuring:** Addressing persistent weakness in the Chinese market, which has been a drag on Water Innovations segment results.
- **CEO Transition:** Board appointed Amit Banati as CEO effective May 13, 2026, succeeding Nicholas Fink (who will resign as CEO and director on April 1, 2026). Banati brings experience from Kellogg/Kellanova.
- **Water Innovation:** Expanding Moen into whole-home water management with filtration, leak detection, and smart water monitoring products.
- **Margin Recovery:** Pursuing cost reduction and productivity programs to restore margins toward historical levels.

## Tariff & Trade Exposure

Fortune Brands has meaningful tariff exposure, particularly in its Water Innovations segment:
- Significant sourcing from China for Moen products and components, directly exposed to U.S.-China tariffs
- Chinese market weakness has been exacerbated by trade tensions
- Some Master Lock and security product manufacturing in Asia
- The company has been actively reshoring and diversifying its supply chain to mitigate tariff impacts

Management has noted tariff-related cost headwinds as a factor in 2026 guidance.

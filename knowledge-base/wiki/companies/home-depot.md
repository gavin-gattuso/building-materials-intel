# The Home Depot Inc.

## Company Overview
The Home Depot is the world's largest home improvement retailer and is aggressively expanding into the professional contractor (Pro) market through its SRS Distribution subsidiary. With over $30 billion in pro-market acquisitions in approximately 18 months, Home Depot is fundamentally reshaping the building products distribution landscape.

## Pro-Market Strategy

### Acquisition Timeline
| Year | Target | Value | Strategic Rationale |
|------|--------|-------|---------------------|
| 2024 | SRS Distribution | ~$18.25B | Roofing, landscaping, exterior products |
| 2025 | GMS Inc. (via SRS) | $5.5B | Drywall, ceilings, steel framing |
| Combined | Total pro-market investment | ~$23.75B | Multi-category pro distribution |

### GMS Deal Details
- Enterprise value: $5.5 billion (including net debt)
- Completed September 2025 (ahead of schedule)
- One of the leading distributors of specialty building products
- Serves both residential and commercial end markets
- Complements SRS Distribution's existing roofing, landscaping, and exterior products

### Market Impact
- Home Depot and Lowe's combined pro-market acquisitions exceed $30 billion in ~18 months
- Traditional independent distributors face intensified competitive pressure
- Specialty distribution consolidation accelerating

## Market Context
- Housing market described as "frozen" by management
- Home improvement demand tepid due to mortgage rate lock-in effect
- Pro customer segment showing relative strength
- Conservative 2026 guidance reflecting macroeconomic uncertainty
- Potential upside if mortgage rates continue declining below 6%

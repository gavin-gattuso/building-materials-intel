# Government Infrastructure Spending

## Current State (2026)
- IIJA + IRA combined: $1.2 trillion authorized
- 2026 is "Shovels in the Ground" peak disbursement year
- 42,105 active projects nationwide
- Heavy Civil solicitations: +40% vs 2024
- Active project growth: +12% YoY

## Major Funding Allocations
- Road/Bridge Repair: $110B
- Water/Wastewater: $55B
- Broadband: $65B

## Distribution Model
- 90% flows through state formula grants
- "Federal money, State rules"

## Risk Factors
- Federal funding cuts/freezes threaten implementation
- NEPA review delays
- Labor shortages constrain execution capacity

## Impact on Building Materials
Infrastructure spending is the strongest demand driver for heavy materials (cement, aggregates, steel rebar, asphalt). Peak disbursement in 2026 supports volumes even if residential weakens.

# Credit Availability & Lending Standards

## Current State (Q4 2025 SLOOS)
- C&I loans: 5-10% of banks tightened (modest)
- CRE (multifamily): modest easing
- CRE (construction/land dev): unchanged overall
- Divergence: large banks eased, smaller banks tightened
- CRE demand: stronger overall

## 2026 Outlook
- Banks expect standards to remain unchanged
- Demand expected to strengthen across all categories
- Banks favoring AI-exposed firms

## Mortgage Market
- Standards: basically unchanged
- Demand: weaker across most categories
- Exception: HELOC demand modestly stronger

## Impact on Building Materials
Stable lending standards with strengthening demand suggests project financing will not be a constraint in 2026. The divergence between large and small banks means larger projects have easier access to credit than smaller regional developments.

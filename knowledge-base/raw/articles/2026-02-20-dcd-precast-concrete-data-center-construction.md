# The Case for Total Precast Concrete Data Center Construction

## Key Points
- Prefabricated precast concrete can accelerate data center construction timelines by 2 to 4 months vs. traditional methods
- Components fabricated off-site in controlled environment and delivered ready for assembly
- Precast concrete offers advantages in durability, fire resistance, and structural performance for data centers
- Method addresses labor shortage challenges by shifting work to factory settings
- Growing adoption as hyperscalers seek faster time-to-market for new capacity
- Trade-offs between steel and concrete approaches being actively evaluated by industry
- Precast approach supports sustainability through reduced waste and optimized material usage

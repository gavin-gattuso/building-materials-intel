# Contractor AI Adoption Surges in 2026, Report Finds

## Key Points
- Artificial intelligence adoption among roofing contractors has surged significantly in 2026
- 40% of contractors currently using AI, up from 29% in 2024
- AI applications include estimating, project management, sales support, and customer communications
- Digital transformation identified by 34% of contractors as top growth opportunity
- Technology adoption seen as critical for managing labor shortages and improving margins
- Companies with higher tech adoption reporting stronger revenue and profit expectations
- AI-powered tools for roof measurement, sales automation, and crew management gaining traction

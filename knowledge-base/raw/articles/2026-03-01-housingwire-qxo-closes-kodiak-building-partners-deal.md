# QXO Closes on $2.25 Billion Kodiak Building Partners Deal

## Key Points
- QXO closed its $2.25 billion acquisition of Kodiak Building Partners
- Deal pushes Brad Jacobs-led distributor deeper into the homebuilding supply chain
- Adds scale in lumber, trusses, and other core structural products
- Moves QXO beyond roofing and exterior products into lumber, trusses, gypsum, and construction supplies
- Also adds fabrication, assembly, and installation capabilities
- QXO backed by $3 billion capital raise completed in January from Apollo and Temasek
- Analysts estimate QXO war chest at around $10 billion for further acquisitions
- Potential future targets include Boise Cascade, BlueLinx Holdings, and US LBM
- Company pursuing aggressive consolidation in the fragmented $800 billion building products sector

# Vulcan Materials: 2026 Looks Attractive With Clear Growth Visibility And Margin Upside

## Key Points
- Vulcan Materials rated attractive for 2026 with clear growth visibility and margin upside potential
- Infrastructure spending from IIJA provides strong multi-year demand visibility for aggregates
- Pricing power remains robust with continued mid-to-high single-digit aggregate price increases expected
- Non-residential construction and public infrastructure spending especially strong in the South and West U.S. regions
- Margin expansion driven by operating leverage on higher volumes and continued price-over-cost spread
- VMC's high-quality aggregate reserves and strategic quarry locations create durable competitive advantages
- Contrasts with the more bearish Hold-rated analysis that sees VMC as somewhat overvalued at current levels

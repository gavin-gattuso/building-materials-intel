# QXO to Acquire Kodiak Building Partners for $2.25 Billion

## Deal Overview
- QXO Inc. announced a definitive agreement to acquire Kodiak Building Partners from Court Square Capital Partners for approximately $2.25 billion on February 10-11, 2026
- Purchase price: $2.0 billion in cash + 13.2 million QXO shares
- QXO retains the right to repurchase shares at $40/share
- Expected close: early Q2 2026 (completed April 1, 2026)

## Kodiak Building Partners Profile
- 2025 revenues: approximately $2.4 billion
- Products: lumber, trusses, windows and doors, construction supplies, waterproofing, roofing, and complementary exterior products
- Services: value-added assembly, fabrication, and installation
- Geographic focus: ~40% of 2025 revenues generated in Florida and Texas
- Florida and Texas building market growth has consistently outpaced national market growth over the last decade

## Strategic Rationale
- Transaction expected to be highly accretive to 2026 earnings
- Expands QXO's current addressable market to more than $200 billion
- Positions QXO as a major player in building products distribution
- Brad Jacobs (QXO CEO) continues strategy of building scale through acquisitions in fragmented building products distribution market

## Industry Significance
- Largest building products distribution deal announced in February 2026
- Follows QXO's prior failed bid for Beacon Roofing Supply
- Signals continued consolidation trend in building products distribution
- Deal valued at approximately 0.94x revenue multiple

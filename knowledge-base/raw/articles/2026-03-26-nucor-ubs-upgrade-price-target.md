# UBS Upgrades Nucor to Buy with $190 Price Target

## Key Details
- **UBS Upgrade**: Nucor upgraded to Buy on March 26, 2026
- **UBS Price Target**: $190.00 (10.17% upside from current price)
- **Analyst Consensus**: "Strong Buy" from 10 analysts
- **Consensus Price Target Range**: $145 to $200
- **Average Price Target**: $175.67 to $180.00 (varies by source)
- **JP Morgan**: Highest target at $200, issued December 5, 2025
- **Wells Fargo**: Lowest target at $145, issued August 14, 2025

## Analysis
- UBS's upgrade to Buy reflects growing confidence in Nucor's earnings trajectory as HRC steel prices push above $1,000/ton
- The upgrade comes ahead of the Apple Grove mill commissioning, which represents a major capacity catalyst
- Analyst consensus has remained broadly bullish on Nucor throughout 2026 despite tariff uncertainty
- The wide price target range ($145-$200) reflects the binary nature of tariff policy risk on domestic steel pricing

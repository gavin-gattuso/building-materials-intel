# Saint-Gobain Reports Full-Year 2025 Results: Sales of EUR 46.5 Billion, EBITDA Up 3.4%

## Key Details
- Full-year 2025 sales grew 2.1% in local currencies to EUR 46.5 billion
- EBITDA reached EUR 7.2 billion, up 3.4% in local currencies; stable margin at 15.5%
- Operating income of EUR 5.29 billion
- Net attributable profit of EUR 2.88 billion; EPS of EUR 5.83, up from EUR 5.64 in 2024
- Free cash flow of EUR 3.8 billion with a 58% conversion ratio
- Proposed dividend of EUR 2.30 per share, up 4.5% from EUR 2.20 in 2024
- 2026 guidance: EBITDA margin above 15%, with H1 affected by extreme weather in Europe and North America
- Q1 2026 sales to be published April 23, 2026

## Analysis
- Saint-Gobain delivered steady performance in a challenging macro environment, with local-currency sales growth and expanding profitability.
- The 15.5% EBITDA margin demonstrates the success of the company's decade-long portfolio transformation toward higher-margin, light-side construction products.
- The cautious 2026 outlook (>15% EBITDA margin vs. 15.5% in 2025) reflects the drag from severe weather in early 2026 and the ongoing European construction downturn.
- With EUR 3.8B in free cash flow and a 58% conversion ratio, Saint-Gobain has significant firepower for its ambitious Lead & Grow capital deployment plan.

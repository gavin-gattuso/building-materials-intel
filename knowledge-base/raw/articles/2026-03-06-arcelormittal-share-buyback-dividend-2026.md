# ArcelorMittal Launches New Share Buyback Program and Raises Dividend for 2026

## Key Details
- **New Buyback Program**: Repurchases to be conducted in tranches through May 2030
- **First Tranche**: Up to 10 million shares, repurchases commencing immediately
- **Capital Returns Policy**: Minimum 50% of post-dividend annual free cash flow returned to shareholders
- **FY 2026 Proposed Dividend**: $0.60/share, up from $0.55/share in FY 2025 (9.1% increase)
- **Significant Shareholder Participation**: Mittal family (holding 44.6% of issued shares) entered share repurchase agreement on March 5, 2026 to sell shares proportionally during the program
- **Annual Report Publication**: Published 2025 Annual Report on Form 20-F on March 6, 2026
- **AGM Date**: May 5, 2026

## Analysis
- The dividend increase and expanded buyback program signal management confidence in ArcelorMittal's cash generation outlook
- The proportional participation of the Mittal family in the buyback is unusual and maintains their ownership percentage while returning capital to all shareholders
- ArcelorMittal's capital returns strategy increasingly positions it as a total return play in the steel sector, competing for investor attention with U.S.-focused peers like Nucor and Steel Dynamics
- The new buyback program's multi-year horizon (through 2030) provides sustained support for share price

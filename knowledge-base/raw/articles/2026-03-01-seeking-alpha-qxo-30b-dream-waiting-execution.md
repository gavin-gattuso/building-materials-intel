# QXO Inc.: A $30 Billion Dream Still Waiting For Execution

## Key Points
- QXO pursuing an ambitious consolidation playbook in the $800 billion building products distribution market under CEO Brad Jacobs
- Kodiak Building Partners acquisition ($2.25B) provides initial platform but larger deals needed to achieve scale
- Beacon Roofing Supply agreed to be acquired by QXO for approximately $11 billion including debt
- QXO initially approached Beacon privately in November with an all-cash bid of $124.25 per share
- Apollo invested $1.2 billion in QXO to fund the acquisition strategy
- Market remains skeptical on whether the rollup strategy can deliver promised returns at scale
- Execution risk remains high as integration of large building products distributors is operationally complex

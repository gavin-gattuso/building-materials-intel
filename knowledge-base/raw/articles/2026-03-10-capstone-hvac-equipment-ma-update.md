# Capstone Partners: HVAC Equipment Sector M&A Update 2026

## Key Findings

- Strong M&A multiples expected to persist in 2026, particularly for participants positioned to capitalize on **data center expansion, electrification, and innovative thermal management systems**
- **Advanced cooling solutions** have become critical to the operation of AI data centers, attracting increased buyer attention to the sector

## Data Center Demand as Key Catalyst

- Demand for cooling solutions derived from **data center construction** has emerged as a key catalyst for product and end market expansion
- Will likely drive **positive M&A momentum through 2026**
- AI data center requirements driving transformational demand for advanced cooling technologies

## Tariff Headwinds

- Key dealmaking headwind: **25% tariff increase on steel and aluminum imports**, raising duty for nearly all trading partners to 50%
- Transactions have primarily focused on **product line expansion and end market diversification** to avoid tariff and supply-chain disruptions

## Notable Transaction

- **RectorSeal** (subsidiary of CSW Industrials, NYSE: CSWI) acquired **Aspen Manufacturing** in March 2025 for **$313.5 million**
- Transaction valued at **2.6x EV/Revenue** and **11.0x EV/EBITDA**

## Strategic Themes

- Buyers who remained active amid geopolitical uncertainty focused on product line expansion
- End market diversification as a strategy to mitigate tariff and supply-chain risks
- Electrification and thermal management driving strategic interest

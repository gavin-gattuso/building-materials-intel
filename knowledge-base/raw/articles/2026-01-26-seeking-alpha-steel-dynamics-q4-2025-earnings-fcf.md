# Steel Dynamics: Free Cash Flow Inflecting, But Valuation Is Full

## Key Points
- Steel Dynamics reported Q4 2025 results with net income falling 24% to $1.19B for full-year 2025, while revenues rose 4% to $18.2B
- STLD trades at 14.5x earnings, viewed as fair value with a Hold rating maintained; analyst prefers pullback below $170 for new entry
- Major growth projects including the Sinton Texas steel mill and new aluminum plant expected to significantly boost profits and cash flow in 2026
- Recent capex cycle completion positions STLD for strong free cash flow with potential for over $2B in FCF and margin expansion ahead
- Potential upside exists if tariffs further tighten markets or buybacks accelerate, but muted demand tempers near-term enthusiasm
- STLD's domestic focus and potential BlueScope acquisition position it as a key beneficiary or casualty of evolving US steel tariffs

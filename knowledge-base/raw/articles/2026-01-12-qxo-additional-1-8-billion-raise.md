# QXO Announces Additional $1.8 Billion Raise for Deal War Chest

## Funding Details

- QXO announced an additional **$1.8 billion** in funding on January 12, 2026
- Investors: **Apollo Global Management**, **Temasek**, and other institutional investors
- Combined with the January 5 raise: total acquisition capital of approximately **$3 billion**
- QXO was in late-stage talks with **7 potential acquisition targets**
- Deadline to announce a deal: **July 15, 2026**

## Timeline of QXO Capital Formation

| Date | Event | Amount |
|------|-------|--------|

## Kodiak Building Partners Profile

- U.S. distributor of lumber, trusses, windows and doors, construction supplies, waterproofing, roofing, and complementary exterior products
- Value-added assembly, fabrication, and installation services
- **2025 Revenue**: approximately **$2.4 billion**
- Previously owned by **Court Square Capital Partners**

## Strategic Context

- QXO targeting the **$200+ billion** building products distribution market
- Brad Jacobs' playbook: acquire platform company, then execute rapid bolt-on M&A
- Building products distribution remains highly fragmented despite recent mega-deals
- QXO positioned alongside Home Depot (SRS), Lowe's (FBM), and CRH as major consolidators
- The rapid capital formation and deal execution underscore investor confidence in the building products sector despite macro headwinds

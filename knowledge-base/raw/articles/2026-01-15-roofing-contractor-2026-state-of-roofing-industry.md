# 2026 State of the Roofing Industry Report

## Key Points
- 2026 roofing industry consensus: stable but uncomfortable due to uncertainties
- 78% of respondents expect sales volumes to increase in 2026; 89% predict increases over next three years
- 75% of surveyed contractors expect revenue growth; 74% anticipate higher profits
- US roofing market on track for steady growth: roughly $31.4 billion in 2025 to over $42 billion by 2030
- Over half of respondents report increasing labor costs, with average labor costs up 14%
- 71% of contractors reported rising wages, up from 55% the previous year
- Labor availability and collections remain the most pressing industry issues
- Commercial roofing expected to see single-digit growth with reroofing making up majority of activity
- AI adoption surged: 40% of contractors using AI in 2025 vs. 29% in 2024

# Waterproof Your Portfolio Using Carlisle Companies

## Key Points
- Carlisle Companies highlighted as a high-quality defensive play in the building envelope sector
- CCM and CWT segments offer recession-resistant revenue from commercial roof replacement and weatherproofing
- Company benefits from aging commercial building stock requiring reroofing and envelope upgrades
- Energy efficiency mandates create regulatory tailwinds for Carlisle's insulation and roofing systems
- Strong execution on Vision 2030 plan targets with consistent margin expansion
- Balance sheet flexibility at 1.4x net debt/EBITDA supports both organic growth and M&A optionality
- Shareholder returns through buybacks and dividend growth provide downside support

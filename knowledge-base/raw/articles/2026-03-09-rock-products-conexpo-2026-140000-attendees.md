# ConExpo-Con/Agg 2026 Attracts More Than 140,000 Attendees

## Key Points
- ConExpo-Con/Agg 2026 attracted more than 140,000 construction professionals from 128 countries
- Show held March 3-7 in Las Vegas, the industry's flagship event held every three years
- More than 2,000 exhibitors across 3 million sq. ft. of exhibit space
- Event brought together aggregates producers, contractors, manufacturers, technology leaders, and workforce advocates
- Showcased new equipment, emerging technologies, and workforce solutions
- Record attendance reflects strong industry interest despite mixed demand signals
- Major aggregates producers and equipment manufacturers unveiled new products and technology

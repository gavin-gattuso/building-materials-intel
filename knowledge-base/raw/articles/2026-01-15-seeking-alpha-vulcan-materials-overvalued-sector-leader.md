# Vulcan Materials: Examining A Somewhat Overvalued Sector Leader

## Key Points
- Vulcan Materials assigned a Hold rating with a fair value estimate of $395 per share
- Analyst suggests a buyable entry point below $285 per share for meaningful margin of safety
- VMC commands a valuation premium justified by market leadership position and high-quality aggregate reserves
- Benefits from robust public infrastructure and non-residential construction spending, particularly in the South and West US regions
- Strong pricing power in aggregates with mid-to-high single-digit price increases expected in 2026
- Infrastructure spending tailwinds from IIJA provide multi-year visibility on demand

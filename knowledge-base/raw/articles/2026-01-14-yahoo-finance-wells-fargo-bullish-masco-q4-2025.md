# Wells Fargo Bullish on Masco Ahead of Q4 2025 Results

## Key Points
- Wells Fargo analyst Sam Reid raised Masco price target from $75.00 to $78.00 and reiterated Overweight rating on January 14, 2026
- Citigroup analyst Anthony Pettinari maintained Neutral rating on Masco but raised price target to $84 from $71 on February 13, 2026
- Masco operates through Plumbing Products (Delta, Hansgrohe) and Decorative Architectural Products segments
- Home improvement and repair & remodel spending expected to gradually recover in 2026
- Strong brand portfolio and recurring renovation demand provide earnings resilience
- Analysts see improving fundamentals ahead of Q4 2025 earnings release

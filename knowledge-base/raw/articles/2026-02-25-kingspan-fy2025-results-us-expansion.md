# Kingspan FY 2025: Revenue EUR 9.2B (+9%), Targets 10% Earnings Growth in 2026

## Key Details
- **FY 2025 Revenue**: EUR 9.2 billion (up 9% at constant currency)
- **FY 2025 EBITDA**: Over EUR 1.2 billion (up 9%)
- **FY 2025 Trading Profit**: EUR 955 million (up 8% at constant currency)
- **FY 2025 EPS**: EUR 3.70
- **FY 2025 Free Cash Flow**: EUR 429 million
- **Year-End Net Debt**: EUR 1.88 billion (net debt/EBITDA: 1.65x)
- **2026 Targets**: ~10% earnings growth; trading profit ~EUR 1.05 billion
- **Effective Tax Rate 2026**: Guided at 16.5%
- **Steico Ownership**: Grew from 51% to 61% in 2025 (largest wood fibre insulation business globally)
- **Nordic Waterproofing**: Took full ownership and completed delisting; total acquisition cost EUR 451.6 million
- **US Roofing Expansion**: $1 billion committed investment over 5 years
  - Oklahoma multi-solution roofing and insulation facility at certification phase
  - Maryland and Oklahoma roofing facilities expected in production in 2026
  - Kentucky plant (larger than Virginia and Arkansas combined) to begin production H2 2026
- **Advances Division**: Expected to reach minimum EUR 300 million EBITDA by 2026 (data center focus)
- **Multiway Acquisition (Jan 22, 2026)**: Acquired Brazilian data center components company

## Analysis
- Kingspan's 9% constant-currency growth in a challenging European construction market demonstrates the strength of its global diversification and exposure to high-growth end markets (data centers, cold storage)
- The $1 billion U.S. investment commitment positions Kingspan as a credible competitor in the North American roofing market, directly competing with established players like Carlisle and QXO/Beacon
- Growing Steico ownership to 61% deepens Kingspan's position in sustainable wood fibre insulation, aligning with tightening building energy codes globally
- The Advances division's EUR 300 million EBITDA target reflects the explosive growth in data center construction spending
- Analyst consensus is Buy with average PT of EUR 87.63 (range EUR 70-130)

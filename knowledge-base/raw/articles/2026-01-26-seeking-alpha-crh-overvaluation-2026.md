# CRH plc: Overvaluation In 2026

## Key Points
- CRH has solid fundamentals but is considered pricey at 22x+ forward P/E with a low dividend yield
- Heavy North America exposure is both a strength and concentration risk
- JP Morgan maintained Overweight rating on CRH on 3/3/2026 and raised price target from $135 to $140
- CRH benefits from strong infrastructure spending tailwinds in the US including IIJA and CHIPS Act funding
- Valuation premium relative to aggregates peers may limit upside from current levels
- Article argues that much of CRH's growth story is already priced in at current valuations

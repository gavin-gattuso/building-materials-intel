# LIXIL Appoints New COO and Chief Impact Officer to Accelerate Growth

## Key Details
- Yugo Kanazawa appointed Chief Operating Officer (COO) and Representative Executive Officer, Executive Vice President, effective April 1, 2026
- Erin McCusker appointed Chief Impact Officer, Executive Officer, Executive Vice President, effective April 1, 2026
- COO role created to enable strategic vision and accelerate change
- Company also announced it is exiting the ceramic siding business by March 2026
- Strategic priorities for 2024-2026: premiumization, smart water tech, Southeast Asia/India expansion, scaling renovation services
- GROHE brand growth in key international markets remains a priority

## Analysis
- The creation of a COO role signals LIXIL's intent to accelerate operational execution after its profit recovery. The exit from ceramic siding represents continued portfolio rationalization toward higher-margin water technology and living products. The Chief Impact Officer role reflects LIXIL's ESG commitment, which is increasingly important in European markets where GROHE competes.

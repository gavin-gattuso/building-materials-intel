# UFP Industries Targets $100M Deckorators Sales Jump in 2026 as Capacity Expands

## Key Points
- UFP Industries targets $100 million increase in Deckorators composite decking sales in 2026
- Q4 2025 net sales reached $1.33 billion with a 7% decline in units and 2% decline in price
- Deckorators specialized installer network has grown to over 900 contractors
- Capital expenditure investments in Northeastern U.S. facilities to meet growing demand
- Shares trade at a reasonable 12-13x earnings multiple offering potential for long-term gains
- Repair and remodeling activity expected to pick up in 2026 supporting wood products demand
- Company executing well on value-added product strategy to diversify beyond commodity lumber

# Martin Marietta Is Still Too Richly Valued Going Into 2026

## Key Points
- Martin Marietta offers strong management execution but faces valuation and cycle risks heading into 2026
- MLM 2026 revenue guidance of $6.42B to $6.78B came in below FactSet consensus estimate of $7.15B
- Premium multiple reflects quality franchise but limits risk-adjusted upside at current prices
- Infrastructure spending from IIJA provides demand floor but residential construction remains headwind
- Aggregates pricing power remains strong with continued mid-single-digit increases expected
- Analyst recommends waiting for a better entry point given stretched valuation multiples

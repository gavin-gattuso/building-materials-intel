# Resilience Drives Growth as Aggregates Leaders Look to 2026

## Key Points
- Aggregates industry leaders express cautious optimism heading into 2026
- Infrastructure investment remains the primary growth driver for aggregate producers
- Estimated total annual production for construction aggregates in 2025 decreased to 2.33 billion metric tons from 2.35 Gt in 2024
- Producers focused on price management and operational efficiency to maintain margins
- Data center construction and energy sector projects providing growing demand
- Public construction activity expected to remain robust through IIJA funding cycle
- Industry resilience demonstrated through ability to overcome adverse weather with pricing discipline

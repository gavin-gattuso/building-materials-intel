# Heidelberg Materials Proposes 9% Dividend Increase to EUR 3.60; Third Buyback Tranche of EUR 450M

## Key Details
- **Proposed dividend**: EUR 3.60 per share for FY2025 (up 9% from EUR 3.30)
- **AGM vote date**: May 13, 2026
- **Share buyback program**: Total EUR 1.2 billion through end of 2026
  - Second tranche (~EUR 400M) acquired June-November 2025 and cancelled in January 2026
  - Third tranche (~EUR 450M) planned to start immediately after AGM
- **Total shareholder return increase**: ~10% higher distributions vs. prior year
- **Adjusted annual profit 2025**: EUR 2.2 billion
- **Analyst price targets**: Jefferies raised to EUR 300 (Buy); JPMorgan raised to EUR 260 (Overweight); Berenberg at EUR 260 (Buy)
- **Consensus**: 15 Buy, 2 Sell, 2 Hold out of 19 analysts

## Analysis
- The combination of 9% dividend growth and a EUR 450M buyback tranche represents a compelling total return story
- The EUR 1.2B total buyback program is meaningfully reducing the outstanding share count, providing EPS accretion
- Heidelberg Materials' capital allocation framework balances growth M&A (Maas Group), shareholder returns, and debt management
- The strong analyst consensus (15 of 19 at Buy) reflects confidence in both the earnings trajectory and shareholder return policy
- Jefferies' EUR 300 target implies significant upside, driven by European cement price recovery expectations

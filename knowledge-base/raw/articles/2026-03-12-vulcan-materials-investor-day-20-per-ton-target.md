# Vulcan Materials Investor Day: Sets Bold $20 Per-Ton Target and $4.5-$5.0B EBITDA Ambition

## Key Details
- **Event date**: March 12, 2026, New York City
- **New cash gross profit per ton target**: $20 (up from $11.33 achieved in 2025)
- **EBITDA target**: $4.5-$5.0 billion at 260-270 million tons
- **Growth since 2022**: 45% increase in aggregates cash gross profit per ton
- **Priority acquisition targets**: 350 million tons identified in U.S. aggregates market (~2.5 billion tons annually)
- **Greenfield pipeline**: 17 projects underway (5 in build-out, 12 in permitting)
- **Greater Charleston/Myrtle Beach**: 9 locations planned by end of 2026
- **Key assumptions**: High single-digit volume growth, low-single-digit to low-double-digit CGP/ton growth, mid-single-digit downstream growth
- **Technology focus**: Innovation and operational efficiency to drive profitability improvement
- **Analyst target post-event**: Street sees $326 average target

## Analysis
- The $20/ton target represents a near-doubling from current levels, signaling management's conviction in structural pricing power
- The $4.5-$5.0B EBITDA ambition would nearly double the 2025 result, implying a multi-year compounding story
- 350 million tons of identified M&A targets suggests a deep pipeline of bolt-on opportunities in the fragmented U.S. aggregates market
- 17 greenfield projects represent organic growth capacity that most competitors cannot easily replicate due to permitting barriers
- The technology and innovation focus suggests Vulcan will invest in automation, AI-driven quarry optimization, and logistics efficiency
- This Investor Day establishes Vulcan's long-term vision and should serve as a catalyst for sustained investor interest

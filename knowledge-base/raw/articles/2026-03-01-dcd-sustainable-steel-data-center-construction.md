# Three Reasons to Build Data Centers with Sustainable Steel

## Key Points
- Growing case for using sustainable (low-carbon) steel in data center construction
- Data centers use huge amounts of concrete and steel, both major CO2 sources
- Steel buildings can be erected and enclosed much faster than concrete data centers
- Modular construction and panelization of steel elements accelerate construction timelines
- Sustainability requirements from hyperscalers (Microsoft, Meta, Google) driving demand for low-carbon steel
- US steel producers with electric arc furnace (EAF) technology well-positioned for low-carbon steel demand
- Material selection becoming critical factor in meeting data center sustainability targets

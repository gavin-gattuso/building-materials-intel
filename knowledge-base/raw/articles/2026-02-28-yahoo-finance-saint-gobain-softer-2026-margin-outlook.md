# Saint-Gobain Shares Down on Softer 2026 Margin Outlook

## Key Points
- Saint-Gobain expects EBITDA margin of more than 15% in 2026, seen as softer than market expectations
- First half of 2026 expected to be affected by extreme weather impacts in Europe and North America
- Stock declined on the guidance release as investors had anticipated stronger margin recovery
- Company remains one of the world's largest building materials manufacturers with operations across construction, renovation, and industrial markets
- European construction recovery remains uneven with residential markets still under pressure
- Saint-Gobain's Lead & Grow strategic plan continues to focus on high-margin renovation and sustainability markets

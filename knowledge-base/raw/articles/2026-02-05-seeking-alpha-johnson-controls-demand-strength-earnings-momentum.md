# Johnson Controls International: Demand Strength Sustains Earnings Momentum

## Key Points
- Johnson Controls raised full-year 2026 adjusted EPS guidance to $4.70 reflecting confidence in sustained demand
- Order growth surged 39% year-over-year with backlog hitting a record $18.2 billion
- Data center expansion is a key growth driver alongside commercial HVAC strength
- Net debt/EBITDA improved to 2.77x with capital allocation focused on debt reduction, dividends, and selective reinvestment
- DCF model supports a Buy rating with $158 target price implying approximately 11% upside
- JCI's transformation, backlog growth, margin improvement, and shareholder-friendly capital allocation driving renewed analyst optimism
- Company outlook calls for fastest earnings growth in a decade

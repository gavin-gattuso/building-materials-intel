# Builders FirstSource Q4 2025: Revenue Falls 12%, Misses Estimates

## Q4 2025 Results
- Net sales: $3.36 billion (down 12.1% YoY)
- Missed analyst revenue expectations by 2.8%
- EPS: $1.12 (missed estimates of $1.24 by $0.12)
- Gross margin: 29.8% (down 250 basis points YoY)
- Adjusted EBITDA: $274.9 million (down 44.3% YoY)
- Adjusted EBITDA margin: 8.2% (contracted 470 bps YoY)

## Full-Year 2025 Results
- Net sales: $15.19 billion (down 7.4% YoY)
- Core organic net sales: down 10.3%
- Second consecutive year of annual sales and earnings declines

## 2026 Guidance
- Net sales: $14.8 billion to $15.8 billion
- Adjusted EBITDA: $1.3 billion to $1.7 billion
- Free cash flow: approximately $500 million

## Key Challenges
- Slow housing starts due to ongoing affordability challenges
- Deflated commodity prices adding to revenue headwinds
- Housing market pressures constraining new home construction volume
- Two consecutive years of annual sales and earnings declines

## Industry Context
- BLDR is the largest U.S. supplier of structural building products to homebuilders
- Results reflect broader weakness in residential construction
- Stock down significantly since earnings release
- Building materials sector overall missed revenue estimates by 1.2% in Q4
- Average share prices across sector down 10.2% since latest earnings results

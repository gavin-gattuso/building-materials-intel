# Carrier Introduces AquaEdge 30CF Chiller for Data Center Reliability and Uptime

## Key Details
- Launched February 26, 2026 as part of Carrier QuantumLeap portfolio
- Air-cooled centrifugal chiller designed specifically for mission-critical data center environments
- Operating temperature range: -20 deg F to 140 deg F
- Can restore 100% cooling in under 3 minutes after power interruption
- Delivers more than 3 MW of cooling capacity depending on ambient conditions
- Uses two-stage, oil-free centrifugal compressor with magnetic bearings
- Backed by expanded global chiller manufacturing capacity
- NVIDIA working with Carrier on cooling designs

## Analysis
- The AquaEdge 30CF is purpose-built for the data center market, addressing the key concern of reliability and rapid restart after power events. The 3-minute full recovery time is a significant differentiator for mission-critical applications. This product launch, combined with Carrier's NVIDIA collaboration, positions the company as a top-tier thermal management partner for hyperscale data centers. The expanded manufacturing capacity ensures Carrier can meet surging demand without supply chain bottlenecks.

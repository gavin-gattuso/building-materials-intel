# ADS Receives Multiple Analyst Upgrades Following Q3 Results

## Key Details
- Consensus rating: "Moderate Buy" from 9 analysts (1 Hold, 8 Buy)
- KeyCorp raised price target from $180 to $198 with "Overweight" rating (February 6, 2026)
- UBS Group set $215 price target with "Buy" rating (February 6, 2026)
- RBC Capital set high-end price target of $205 (February 6, 2026)
- Average consensus price target of $186.00
- Earlier, KeyCorp had raised from $170 to $180 in January 2026

## Analysis
- The wave of analyst upgrades following Q3 results reflects growing confidence in ADS's market position and margin trajectory. The $215 UBS target implies significant upside and recognition of ADS's unique positioning at the intersection of infrastructure spending and water management. ADS is increasingly viewed as a secular growth story within building materials.

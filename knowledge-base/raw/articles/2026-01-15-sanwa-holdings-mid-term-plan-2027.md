# Sanwa Holdings Mid-Term Management Plan 2027: JPY 50 Billion M&A Target

## Key Details
- Mid-Term Management Plan covers 2025-2027 ("Global Vision 2030")
- M&A investment target of JPY 50.0 billion (~$340 million)
- North America: expand business domain through Overhead Door Corporation
- Europe: expand industrial doors and service businesses
- Japan: expand product diversification and digitalization
- Shareholder returns enhanced with new share repurchase program and increased dividends
- Focus on strengthening service business for recurring revenue

## Analysis
- Sanwa Holdings' JPY 50 billion M&A target represents a meaningful acceleration of inorganic growth. As the world's largest manufacturer of shutters and doors (through subsidiaries Overhead Door, Novoferm, and Sanwa Shutter), the company is well-positioned to consolidate fragmented regional markets. The emphasis on service business expansion could improve recurring revenue and margin quality, similar to strategies adopted by other building products companies.

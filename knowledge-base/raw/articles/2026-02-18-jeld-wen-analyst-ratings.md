# JELD-WEN Analyst Consensus at Hold as Price Targets Diverge Widely

## Key Details
- Consensus rating: Hold from 7 analysts
- Average price target: $4.61 (implying 78% upside from recent price)
- Goldman Sachs lowered price target from $3.75 to $3.25
- Median price target from 21 analysts: $2.55
- Highest price target: $3.50; lowest: $2.00
- FY2026 revenue estimate revised down to $3.29 billion from prior $3.40 billion

## Analysis
- The extreme divergence in JELD-WEN price targets (from $2.00 to $4.61) reflects deep uncertainty about the company's trajectory. Goldman's $3.25 target and the lowered revenue estimates suggest limited confidence in a near-term recovery. The stock is essentially a binary bet on whether the North American housing market recovers and whether the European strategic review yields a positive outcome.

# Trane Technologies Completes Acquisition of LiquidStack to Accelerate Data Center Thermal Management

## Key Details
- Acquisition announced February 10, 2026; completed March 3, 2026
- LiquidStack is a global leader in liquid cooling technology for data centers (HQ: Carrollton, Texas)
- Specializes in direct-to-chip and immersion cooling solutions
- Solutions engineered for generative AI and hyperscale computing demands
- Builds on Trane Technologies' minority investment in LiquidStack in 2023
- Enhances end-to-end data center thermal management: chillers, heat rejection, controls, liquid distribution, on-chip cooling
- Includes LiquidStack's global team and manufacturing/engineering/R&D in Texas and Hong Kong
- LiquidStack operates within Commercial HVAC business unit of Trane Americas segment
- Financial terms not disclosed

## Analysis
- The LiquidStack acquisition gives Trane Technologies the critical liquid cooling capability needed to serve the highest-density AI data center racks. By combining LiquidStack's direct-to-chip and immersion cooling with Trane's existing chiller, heat rejection, and controls portfolio, Trane now offers a complete thermal chain from chip to atmosphere. The progression from minority investment (2023) to full acquisition (2026) shows a deliberate, de-risked approach to entering this market. This positions Trane alongside Carrier and Johnson Controls in the data center HVAC race.

# US Cement Surge Outpaces Europe as Divergence Widens

## Key Points
- United States remains by far the most attractive and strategically decisive market for global cement producers
- Unique combination of infrastructure stimulus, structural under-supply, and resilient pricing sets US apart
- US end-markets expected to deliver strongest margin expansion globally in 2026
- CEMEX and Heidelberg Materials stand out: CEMEX through operational efficiencies and disciplined capital allocation; Heidelberg through decarbonization monetization
- Both companies shifted toward businesses aligned with US demand including aggregates and downstream value-added materials
- Holcim, now more Europe-weighted, faces slower growth and greater regulatory exposure
- CRH advanced decarbonization strategy with $2.1 billion Eco Materials Technologies acquisition, consolidating its American profile
- European cement markets facing slower growth, higher regulatory costs, and policy uncertainty

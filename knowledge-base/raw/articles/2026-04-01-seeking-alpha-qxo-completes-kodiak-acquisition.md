# QXO Completes Acquisition of Kodiak Building Partners

## Key Points
- QXO completed its $2.25 billion acquisition of Kodiak Building Partners on April 1, 2026
- Purchase price comprises $2.0 billion cash and 13.2 million QXO shares, with QXO retaining right to repurchase shares at $40
- Kodiak generated approximately $2.4 billion in revenues in 2025 as a distributor of lumber, trusses, windows, doors, and construction supplies
- Transaction expected to be highly accretive to 2026 earnings per Brad Jacobs, Chairman and CEO
- Expands QXO's addressable market to more than $200 billion in building products distribution
- Benchmark reaffirmed Buy rating on QXO following the Kodiak deal completion
- QXO pursuing aggressive consolidation strategy in the fragmented building products distribution market

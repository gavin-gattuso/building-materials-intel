# AGC Inc Completes Exit from Small Glass Substrate Business for Mobile Phones

## Key Details
- AGC Corporation completed restructuring initiative exiting the small-glass-substrate business for mobile phones
- Marks a pivotal step toward corporate recovery and portfolio focus
- Part of the broader "AGC plus-2026 Strategy" announced February 2024
- Strategy aims to accelerate corporate transformation and maximize returns
- Focus areas: improving profitability through business restructuring, strengthening competitiveness through technological innovation, revising pricing policies
- AGC increasing production capacity in Thailand to capture Southeast Asian demand
- Company targeting Vision 2030 milestones through the restructuring program

## Analysis
- The exit from mobile glass substrates reflects AGC's shift away from commoditized consumer electronics components toward higher-value architectural, automotive, and display glass segments.
- This restructuring is consistent with the broader industry trend of glass manufacturers rationalizing portfolios to focus on specialty, high-margin products.
- Increased Thailand capacity signals AGC's bet on Southeast Asian construction and automotive markets as growth drivers, particularly as China's construction market matures.
- The move supports AGC's FY2026 operating profit target of JPY 150 billion by eliminating underperforming business lines.

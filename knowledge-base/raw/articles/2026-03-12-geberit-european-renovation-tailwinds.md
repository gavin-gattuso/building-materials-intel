# Geberit Faces Margin Pressure as European Renovation Provides Tailwind

## Key Details
- European renovation market accounts for 70% of Geberit's volumes
- New construction remains subdued across Europe, particularly in Germany
- German ceramics plant closure completed (EUR 24M one-off impact)
- 2026 outlook: consensus expects 5% local-currency revenue growth
- Analysts divided: 4 Buy vs. 5 Sell ratings, overall Neutral consensus
- Stock trades at approximately 30x FY26 earnings estimate
- Average analyst forecast for 2026 net profit: CHF 674 million

## Analysis
- Geberit's heavy renovation exposure is both a strength and a limitation. While renovation provides resilient demand (homeowners continue to upgrade bathrooms even in downturns), it lacks the explosive growth potential of a new construction recovery. The margin pressure from rising personnel costs and one-off restructuring charges will need to moderate for the stock to outperform. The neutral analyst consensus reflects this balanced risk-reward.

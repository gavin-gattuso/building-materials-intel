# Advanced Drainage Systems Reports Strong Q2 FY2026 with 8.7% Revenue Growth

## Key Details
- Q2 FY2026 net sales of $850.4 million, up 8.7% ($67.8 million increase)
- Net income of $156.5 million, up 19.3% ($25.3 million increase)
- EPS of $1.99, up 19.2%
- Adjusted EBITDA of $287.5 million, up 17.1% ($42.0 million increase)
- Q2 represented strongest quarter of FY2026

## Analysis
- The 8.7% revenue growth in Q2 was driven by favorable construction activity in the summer months and continued market share gains. The 17% EBITDA growth on 9% revenue growth demonstrates strong operating leverage. ADS benefits from the increasing regulatory focus on stormwater management and green infrastructure, which creates sustained demand regardless of the broader housing cycle.

# KPMG: Slight Blip Up in Construction -- December 2025 Construction Spending

## Key Findings

- December 2025 construction spending saw a modest uptick following prior monthly softness
- Data provided context for the broader trend of uneven construction activity heading into 2026
- Public spending continued to support overall levels while private sectors remained mixed
- The report set the stage for KPMG's subsequent January 2026 analysis showing further weakening

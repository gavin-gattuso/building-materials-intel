# Martin Marietta Sets Fourth-Quarter Records in Building Materials

## Key Points
- Martin Marietta posted record fourth-quarter results in its building materials segment
- 2026 shipment guidance reflects low single-digit improvement
- Expectations for robust infrastructure investment and accelerating momentum in data centers and energy
- Data center and energy demand expected to offset continued softness in private nonresidential and residential construction
- Martin Marietta acquired aggregate and asphalt assets in Minnesota from CRH
- Minnesota acquisition adds approximately 40 million tons of aggregate reserves
- Expanded footprint in Minneapolis, St. Paul, and St. Cloud markets
- Pricing discipline continues to drive margin improvement

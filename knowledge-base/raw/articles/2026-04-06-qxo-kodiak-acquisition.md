# QXO Completes $2.25 Billion Acquisition of Kodiak Building Partners

## Deal Details
- Purchase price: $2.25 billion
- Structure: $2 billion cash + 13,157,895 shares of QXO common stock
- Seller: Court Square Capital Partners
- Close date: April 1, 2026
- QXO retains right to repurchase shares at $40/share

## Kodiak Profile
- 2025 revenue: ~$2.4 billion
- Products: lumber, trusses, windows, doors, construction supplies, waterproofing, roofing
- Services: assembly, fabrication, installation

## Strategic Context
- QXO's second major acquisition (first: Beacon Roofing Supply for $11B, closed April 2025)
- Expands QXO addressable market to $200+ billion
- Target: $50 billion annual revenue within the next decade
- CEO: Brad Jacobs

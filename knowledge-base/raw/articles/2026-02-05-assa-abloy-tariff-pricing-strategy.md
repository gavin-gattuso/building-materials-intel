# ASSA ABLOY to Offset Tariff Impact Through Price Hikes, CEO Says

## Key Details
- CEO Nico Delvaux confirmed ASSA ABLOY is compensating tariff costs through price increases
- 2026 tariff price adjustment set at 6.25% on US products (per price book effective February 2, 2026)
- Company targeting overall pricing of 1.5-2% globally for 2026
- Expected volume growth of 2-3% in 2026
- Company exploring dual-sourcing strategy: producing products in at least two locations as backup
- EBIT margin target within 16-17% range for 2026
- Multiple price increases already implemented

## Analysis
- ASSA ABLOY's ability to pass through tariff costs via pricing reflects its market-leading position in access solutions. The dual-sourcing strategy is a longer-term supply chain de-risking move that other building materials companies are also pursuing. The 6.25% US tariff surcharge is significant and will flow through to contractors and end users.

# Masco Announces Q1 2026 Earnings Date: April 22, Analysts Expect EPS of $0.88

## Key Details
- Q1 2026 earnings release scheduled for April 22, 2026
- Analyst consensus EPS estimate: $0.88, a modest increase from prior year
- Company expects margin contraction in H1 2026 before improvement in H2
- Full-year guidance remains $4.10-$4.30 EPS

## Analysis
- The Q1 2026 report will be an important data point for assessing whether Masco's tariff mitigation actions are tracking to plan and whether repair and remodel demand is stabilizing. The expected H1 margin pressure means Q1 could show compressed profitability, making the quality of management commentary on order trends particularly important for building materials investors.

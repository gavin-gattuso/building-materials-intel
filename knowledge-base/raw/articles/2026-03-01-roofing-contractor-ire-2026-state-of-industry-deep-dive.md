# IRE 2026 General Session: A Deep Dive into the State of the Industry Report

## Key Points
- International Roofing Expo 2026 featured deep dive into state of the roofing industry
- Diesel prices reached $5.40 per gallon, up more than $1.90 since start of 2026
- Elevated diesel directly increases mobilization, crane use, and material delivery costs
- Impact especially significant for membrane rolls, insulation, and ballast deliveries
- Construction hiring improving but fuel costs and borrowing rates eating into good news
- Rising labor and overhead costs, material price increases, and workforce shortages challenging margins
- Industry leaders sizing up 2026 outlook and strategies for winning in uncertain environment

# Holcim Full-Year 2025 Results: Record 18.3% EBIT Margin Post-Amrize Spinoff

## Key Details
- **Total sales 2025**: CHF 15.72 billion (down 2.9% YoY, reflecting Amrize spinoff impact)
- **Recurring EBIT**: CHF 2.88 billion (up 1.4% YoY)
- **Recurring EBIT margin**: 18.3% (industry-leading, double-digit growth)
- **Free cash flow**: CHF 2.154 billion (up 1.6% YoY)
- **Cash conversion ratio**: 54%
- **Net income**: CHF 387 million (down 73% YoY due to spinoff-related one-time items)
- **Amrize spinoff completed**: June 23, 2025 (dividend-in-kind, 1 AMRZ share per HOLN share)
- **Amrize 2024 revenue**: $11.7 billion; $3.7 billion adjusted EBITDA
- **2026 Guidance**: Organic sales growth 3-5%; organic recurring EBIT growth 8-10%; FCF ~CHF 2 billion

## Analysis
- Holcim's post-spinoff results demonstrate that the remaining European/LatAm/Asia-Pacific business is a high-margin, capital-efficient operation
- The 18.3% recurring EBIT margin is industry-leading among global cement majors
- The 73% net income decline is a non-cash accounting artifact of the Amrize spinoff and should not be interpreted as operational deterioration
- The 2026 guidance of 8-10% organic EBIT growth signals management confidence in European market recovery
- Post-spinoff, Holcim is a more focused company with clearer European and emerging market exposure
- The Amrize separation allows both entities to pursue independent capital allocation and M&A strategies

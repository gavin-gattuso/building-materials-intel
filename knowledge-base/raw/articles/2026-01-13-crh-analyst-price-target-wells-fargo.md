# CRH Analyst Consensus: Strong Buy with $140+ Price Targets

## Key Details
- **Wells Fargo** (Jan 13, 2026): Raised price target from $133 to $138, maintained Overweight
- **JP Morgan** (Mar 3, 2026): Set price target at $140.00, Overweight rating, implying 36% upside potential
- **Consensus rating**: Buy, based on 13 analysts as of March 31, 2026
- **Average price target**: $141.99 (range: $108.07 to $171.15)
- **CEO transition**: Jim Mintern succeeded Albert Manifold as CEO effective January 1, 2025
- **CFO change**: Nancy Buese became CFO on May 12, 2025 (previously CFO of Baker Hughes and Newmont)

## Analysis
- The unanimous Buy consensus reflects confidence in CRH's North America-centric strategy and infrastructure-driven demand
- The leadership transition has been well-received by the Street, with no disruption to the growth strategy
- Price targets imply significant upside, driven by expectations for IIJA spending acceleration and continued M&A execution
- CRH's dual listing (NYSE and LSE) and S&P 500 inclusion continue to attract institutional flows
- The wide price target range ($108-$171) reflects uncertainty around infrastructure policy post-IIJA expiration in September 2026

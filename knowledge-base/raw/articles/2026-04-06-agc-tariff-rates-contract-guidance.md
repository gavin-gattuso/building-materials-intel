# Construction Tariffs 2026: AGC Urges Contract Updates Amid Surging Steel, Copper, Lumber Costs

## Current Tariff Rates (Effective April 2, 2026)
- Steel, aluminum, copper: 50%
- Steel/aluminum/copper derivatives: 25%
- Industrial/electrical grid equipment containing these metals: 15%
- Foreign-made products using only American metals: 10%
- Softwood lumber and timber: 10%
- Lumber derivatives: 25%
- Global baseline tariff: 10% (expires July 2026)

## AGC Contract Recommendations
1. Include escalation provisions (ConsensusDocs 200.1)
2. Document baseline pricing at contract signing
3. Build contingency language for tariff rate changes

## Forward Risks
- Additional tariffs under investigation: critical minerals, robotics, semiconductors, heavy trucks
- Domestic materials not price-safe: "demand due to tariffs" fluctuates domestic pricing

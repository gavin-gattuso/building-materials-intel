# Masco Reports 2025 Results: Net Sales Down 3%, Guides 2026 EPS $4.10-$4.30

## Key Details
- Full-year 2025 net sales down 3% (down 2% excluding currency and Kichler divestiture)
- Q4 2025 EPS of $0.80
- Full-year adjusted operating profit of approximately $1.3 billion, 16.8% operating margin
- Full-year adjusted EPS of $3.96
- Return on invested capital: 41%
- 2026 guidance: sales flat to up low-single digits, operating margin expanding to ~17%
- 2026 EPS guidance: $4.10-$4.30
- Diluted share count assumption: 202 million; effective tax rate: 24.5%
- Board declared $0.32/share quarterly dividend
- New $2.0 billion share repurchase authorization (effective February 10, 2026, replacing existing program)
- Expects margin contraction in H1 2026, expansion in H2 as tariff mitigation takes hold
- Stock surged 7.7% on earnings beat

## Analysis
- Masco's results and guidance paint a picture of disciplined execution in a soft market. The 16.8% operating margin and 41% ROIC demonstrate the quality of Masco's plumbing (Delta, Hansgrohe) and decorative architectural (Behr paint) franchises. The $2B buyback authorization is aggressive and signals confidence in free cash flow generation. The H1/H2 margin profile is important for investors to understand the tariff timing dynamics.

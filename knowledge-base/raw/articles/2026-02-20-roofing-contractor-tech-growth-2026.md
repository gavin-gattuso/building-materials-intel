# Roofing Contractors Bet on Tech for Growth in 2026

## Key Points
- AI adoption surging among roofing contractors: 40% currently using AI vs. 29% in 2024
- Contractors identified digital transformation (34%) as key growth opportunity alongside customization (40%) and trade expansion (36%)
- Owens Corning highlighted tech stack for end-to-end contractor support
- Owens Corning innovations include VR-enabled OC Craft training and Roof Scout AI-powered virtual sales agent
- Technology adoption seen as critical differentiator in tight-margin environment
- Digital tools helping address labor shortage through efficiency gains and training
- Survey data shows strong correlation between tech adoption and revenue growth expectations

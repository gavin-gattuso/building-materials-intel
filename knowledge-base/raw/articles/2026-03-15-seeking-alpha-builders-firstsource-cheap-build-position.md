# Builders FirstSource Stock: Nice To Build A Position While It's Cheap

## Key Points
- BLDR looks undervalued despite housing headwinds with strong FCF and EBITDA guidance supporting a 2026 rebound thesis
- Trading at multi-year low valuation multiples offers an attractive entry point for long-term investors
- M&A optionality provides additional upside as BLDR has capacity to make accretive acquisitions
- Housing market expected to gradually recover in 2H 2026 as interest rates potentially ease
- BLDR's value-added product mix including trusses, millwork, and windows provides margin resilience
- Company maintains strong competitive position as the largest U.S. supplier of building products to homebuilders

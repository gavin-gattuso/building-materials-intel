# LIXIL Core Earnings Up 17% in Q3 FYE2026 Despite Flat Revenue

## Key Details
- Q3 FYE2026 revenue declined marginally YoY to JPY 1,138.5 billion
- Core earnings increased 17.5% to JPY 36.5 billion
- Japan revenue increased 1% YoY to JPY 779.1 billion
- International revenue decreased 2% to JPY 385.1 billion (down 3% ex-FX)
- Net profit improved by JPY 7.5 billion YoY to JPY 11.8 billion
- H1 FYE2026 core earnings surged 60% despite revenue dip
- Q1 FYE2026 core earnings soared 15-fold
- LIXIL Water Technology international saw strong performance in Europe, Middle East, and India
- U.S. and China demand remained sluggish
- Renovation products drove robust Japan performance
- CEO Kinya Seto: "We are not going after market share at the lower end. We want to increase the average price."

## Analysis
- LIXIL's profit recovery story is dramatic: from 15x earnings improvement in Q1 to sustained 17% growth in Q3. The premiumization strategy is working, particularly in Japan's renovation market and international water technology. The geographic divergence -- strong Europe/India vs. weak US/China -- mirrors broader building materials trends. LIXIL's focus on higher average selling prices rather than volume growth is a defensive but margin-accretive strategy.

# CRH Completes $2.1 Billion Eco Material Technologies Acquisition -- Reshaping Cement Industry

## Deal Summary

- **Acquirer**: CRH plc
- **Target**: Eco Material Technologies
- **Value**: **$2.1 billion** (all cash)
- **Announced**: July 29, 2025
- **Completed**: September 22, 2025
- No expected change to CRH credit ratings

## Eco Material Technologies Profile

- **Headquarters**: Utah
- Leading supplier of Supplementary Cementitious Materials (SCMs) in North America
- National network of fresh and harvested fly ash, pozzolans, synthetic gypsum, and green cement operations
- Over **125** utility source locations, production facilities, and terminals

## Strategic Significance

- Positions CRH at the forefront of transition to next-generation cement and concrete
- Addresses growing demand for cementitious products to modernize North American infrastructure
- SCMs reduce the carbon footprint of concrete -- a key sustainability play
- Fly ash and pozzolans partially replace Portland cement, reducing CO2 emissions

## CRH in 2026

- 2026 earnings estimates: **$5.56 per share**, revenue of **$37.57 billion**
- Expected EPS growth of **3.15%**, flat revenue YoY
- CRH is one of the world's largest building materials companies
- The aggregates in road construction market expected to reach **$1.67 trillion** by 2030

## Cement Market Context

- Top six cement players control approximately **65-70%** of US capacity
- 25% tariffs on Mexican and Canadian cement adding to domestic pricing power
- EPA kiln regulations driving 4-6% cement price increases
- Infrastructure demand via IIJA providing stable demand floor

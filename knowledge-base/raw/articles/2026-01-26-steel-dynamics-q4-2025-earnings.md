# Steel Dynamics Q4 2025: Record Shipments, EPS Beats Despite Softer Prices

## Key Details
- **Q4 2025 EPS**: $1.82 (beat analyst estimate of $1.70 by 7.06%)
- **Q4 2025 Net Sales**: $4.4 billion
- **Q4 2025 Net Income**: $266 million
- **Q3 2025 Comparison**: Net income of $404 million ($2.74/share)
- **Q4 2024 Comparison**: Net income of $207 million ($1.36/share)
- **Full-Year 2025 Net Income**: $1.2 billion ($7.99/share)
- **Full-Year 2025 Net Sales**: $18.2 billion
- **Full-Year 2025 Operating Income**: $1.5 billion
- **Full-Year 2025 Adjusted EBITDA**: $2.2 billion
- **Share Repurchases**: $900 million of common stock repurchased in 2025
- **Record Achievement**: Record steel shipments in 2025
- **Q1 2026 Guidance**: EPS of $2.73 to $2.77 per diluted share (50% sequential increase)

## Analysis
- Steel Dynamics delivered record steel shipments despite a challenging pricing environment in H2 2025
- The sharp sequential EPS improvement guided for Q1 2026 ($2.73-$2.77 vs $1.82 in Q4) reflects the domestic steel price recovery driven by tariff protections
- The $900 million in share buybacks demonstrates management's commitment to returning capital through the cycle
- STLD's integrated steel-aluminum platform is becoming a competitive differentiator as the aluminum mill ramps to commercial production

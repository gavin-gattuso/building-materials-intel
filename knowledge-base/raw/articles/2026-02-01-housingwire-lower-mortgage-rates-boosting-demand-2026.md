# Lower Mortgage Rates Boosting Demand Early in 2026

## Key Points
- Lower mortgage rates in early 2026 leading to increased housing demand
- Both pending home sales and purchase applications posting week-over-week and year-over-year gains
- Mortgage rates expected to remain in the low-6% range through 2026
- Affordability showing signs of improvement with rates trending down
- NAHB chief economist forecasts modest 1% rise in housing starts for 2026
- Renewed buyer interest building momentum for spring housing season
- Demand improvement positive for building materials consumption if sustained

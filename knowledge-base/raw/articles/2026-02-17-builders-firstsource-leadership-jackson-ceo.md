# Builders FirstSource Leadership: CEO Peter Jackson's First Full Reporting Year; Digital Strategy Advances

## Key Details
- **CEO**: Peter Jackson (effective November 6, 2024)
- **Previous Role**: Chief Financial Officer of BLDR
- **Experience**: 30 years leadership; 17 years in building products; 7 years at BLDR
- **CFO**: Pete Beckmann (succeeded Jackson as CFO)
- **New Hire**: Gayatri Narayan appointed President, Technology and Digital Solutions (June 2025) -- newly created role
- **Strategic Focus**: Growth strategy, capital allocation, M&A, digital transformation, business intelligence
- **Predecessor**: Dave Rush (retired after 25 years; CEO since Nov 2022)

## Analysis
- Peter Jackson's Q4 2025 earnings report represents his first full quarter as CEO, establishing his leadership during a challenging period for the housing market
- The creation of a President of Technology and Digital Solutions role signals BLDR's strategic pivot toward technology-enabled distribution, potentially responding to QXO's tech-forward narrative
- Jackson's CFO background suggests a capital allocation-focused leadership style, which is appropriate given BLDR's massive buyback program and the need for disciplined investment through the housing downcycle
- The leadership transition occurred at a natural inflection point: BLDR faces both cyclical headwinds (housing) and structural opportunities (value-added products, digital tools)

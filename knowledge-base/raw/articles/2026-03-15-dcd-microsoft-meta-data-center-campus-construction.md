# Hyperscaler Data Center Construction Boom Continues in 2026

## Key Points
- Microsoft broke ground on data center campuses in Cheyenne (Wyoming), Cherry Valley (Illinois), Wisconsin ($1B), and North Carolina
- Microsoft also paused construction on part of Mount Pleasant, Wisconsin site, signaling selective approach
- Meta estimating 2026 full-year capex of $115-135 billion, with plans to spend $600B on US data centers by 2028
- Meta developing $10 billion data center in Richland Parish, Louisiana (4 million sq ft, 1.5GW by end of 2027)
- Meta investing $800 million in Aiken County, South Carolina campus
- Meta announced 1GW data center campus in Indiana
- Meta piloted mass-timber construction on US data center campuses starting with South Carolina admin building
- Microsoft signed cement supply agreements with low-carbon cement producer Sublime
- Microsoft planning Northern Virginia data centers using hybrid cross-laminated timber, steel, and concrete

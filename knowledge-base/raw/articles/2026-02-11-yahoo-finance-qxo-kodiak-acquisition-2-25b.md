# QXO Announces $2.25B Acquisition of Kodiak Building Partners

## Key Points
- QXO Inc. announced definitive agreement to acquire Kodiak Building Partners for approximately $2.25 billion
- Purchase comprises $2.0 billion cash and 13.2 million QXO shares with repurchase right at $40 per share
- Kodiak generated approximately $2.4 billion in 2025 revenues distributing lumber, trusses, windows, doors, and construction supplies
- Transaction expands QXO's addressable market to more than $200 billion
- Benchmark reaffirmed Buy rating on QXO following the deal announcement
- Deal is the first major platform acquisition in Brad Jacobs' building products rollup strategy
- Transaction expected to be highly accretive to 2026 earnings

# Builders FirstSource: The Margin Floor Is In, Building Back Higher In 2026

## Key Points
- BLDR's margins appear to have bottomed with management guiding FY25 EBITDA of $1.625-1.675 billion and free cash flow of $900 million +/- $100 million
- 2026 net sales guidance of $14.8 billion to $15.8 billion from MarketScreener data
- Valuation is compelling at multi-year lows with P/B at 2.21x and P/S at 0.6x, suggesting attractive upside potential
- Topline contraction persisting since Q1 2023 expected to continue in H1 2026 before recovery
- Analyst sees BLDR as a buy offering compelling risk-reward for patient investors ahead of potential 2026 sector rebound
- Strong free cash flow generation supports buybacks and debt reduction even in a soft housing environment

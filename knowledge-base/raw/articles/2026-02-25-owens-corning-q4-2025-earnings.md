# Owens Corning Q4 2025 Earnings: Revenue Miss but Capital Returns Strong

## Q4 2025 Results (Reported February 25, 2026)

- **Net Sales**: $2.14 billion, down **17%** from $2.57 billion in prior-year period
- Slightly below forecast of $2.17 billion
- **Adjusted EPS**: $1.10, missing consensus estimate of $1.36 by approximately **19%**

## Full Year 2025 Results

- **Revenue**: $10.1 billion, up a modest **3%** from 2024
- Full-year results reflected integration of Masonite acquisition

## Q1 2026 Guidance

- Revenue projected at approximately **$2.1 billion to $2.2 billion**
- Adjusted EBITDA margins expected in the **mid-teens**
- Cautious outlook reflecting macro uncertainty

## Segment Analysis

- **Roofing**: Core segment facing volume pressures from housing slowdown
- **Insulation**: Benefiting from energy efficiency mandates and code upgrades
- **Composites**: Serving wind energy and industrial markets

## Capital Returns

- Despite earnings miss, capital returns to shareholders remained strong
- Share buyback and dividend programs continued

## Market Context

- Building materials sector overall underperformed in Q4, missing revenue estimates by 1.2%
- Average share prices down 10.2% since latest earnings results across the sector
- Cyclical pressures from interest rates weighing on construction volumes

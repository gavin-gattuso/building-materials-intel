# Six Trends in Aggregates for 2026

## Key Points
- Pit & Quarry identifies six major trends shaping the aggregates industry heading into 2026
- Infrastructure market remains strong, giving producers firm footing as they start the year
- Nonresidential megaprojects including data centers, LNG construction, and manufacturing facilities are providing supplemental demand
- IIJA-funded projects moving into full execution phase, driving aggregate consumption
- Private residential and nonresidential construction remain soft due to elevated interest rates
- Pricing discipline continues across the industry, with producers maintaining margins despite volume headwinds
- Industry consolidation accelerating as major producers seek strategic acquisitions

# 3 Forces Shaping the Housing Market in 2026

## Key Points
- Three key forces identified shaping the 2026 housing market: affordability trends, mortgage rate trajectory, and policy/regulatory environment
- Housing starts forecast to rise approximately 3% in 2026 after a 7% decline in 2025
- Affordability improving as wage growth outpaces inflation and home prices moderate
- Mortgage rates have trended down by half a point over recent months
- NAHB/Wells Fargo Housing Market Index fell to 36 in February, reflecting builder caution
- Elevated land and construction costs continue to weigh on builder optimism
- About 300,000-person shortage of skilled construction labor persists
- Regulatory costs add approximately $94,000 per single-family home

# UFP Industries FY2026 Earnings Forecasts Issued; Moderate Buy Consensus

## Key Details
- Consensus rating: Moderate Buy to Hold (1 Buy, 2 Hold depending on source)
- DA Davidson: Buy rating, price target lowered to $110 from $112
- Average 12-month price target: $106.67-$111.80
- Price target range: $95.00 - $135.00 (widest range among sources)
- Median price target: $111.00 from 9 analysts
- FY2026 earnings estimates being issued by brokerages
- Company trades at a premium to lumber peers given Deckorators growth profile

## Analysis
- The Hold-leaning consensus for UFP Industries reflects the tension between strong Deckorators growth and persistent weakness in the legacy lumber/site-built housing business.
- The premium valuation relative to pure lumber plays (West Fraser, Canfor, Interfor) is justified by UFP's diversified business model and composite decking growth trajectory.
- The $95-$135 price target range (42% spread) reflects significant disagreement about how quickly Deckorators can offset lumber market headwinds.
- With $2.2B in liquidity and minimal debt, UFP has optionality to accelerate growth through M&A if valuations become more attractive in a continued downturn.

# Tariffs Could Inflate Housing Costs, but the Full Impact Remains Uncertain

## Key Points
- Tariff increases threatening to raise building materials costs for homebuilders
- Lumber and steel among materials most exposed to tariff-driven price increases
- Full impact on housing costs remains uncertain as supply chains adjust
- Tariffs compounding existing affordability challenges in the housing market
- Homebuilders may face difficult choices between absorbing costs or passing them to buyers
- Canadian lumber imports particularly exposed given existing 25% tariff framework
- Construction materials supply chain disruptions could extend project timelines
- Industry groups warning that tariffs will further constrain already-tight housing supply

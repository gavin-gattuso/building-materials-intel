# USA Raises Vietnam Cement Import Tariffs to 20%

## Key Points
- US increased tariffs on cement imported from Vietnam to 20%
- Additional tariffs on April 2 revealed ranging from 10-50% on various countries
- 20% tariff imposed on imports from the European Union
- Tariff actions part of broader trade policy affecting building materials supply chains
- Import tariffs expected to tighten domestic cement supply and support domestic pricing
- Cement demand outlook for 2026 projected at only 1.1% recovery after 3.9% decline in 2025
- American Cement Association (ACA) warns of tariff fallout as cement demand outlook weakens

# Heidelberg Materials Closes 2025 Financial Year with Record Result

## Key Details
- **Revenue**: EUR 21.5 billion, up 1% YoY
- **Result from Current Operations (RCO)**: EUR 3.4 billion, up 6% like-for-like (record high)
- **RCOBD margin**: 21.8% (vs. 21.3% prior year)
- **Adjusted EPS**: EUR 12.41, up 4% YoY (EUR 11.94 in 2024)
- **Adjusted EPS CAGR since 2020**: 12%
- **Share buyback**: Second tranche of EUR 1.2 billion program completed; shares cancelled in January 2026
- **2026 Guidance**: RCO between EUR 3.40-3.75 billion; ROIC above 10%
- **Consensus estimate**: EUR 3.66 billion RCO for 2026
- Results announced February 25, 2026

## Analysis
- Heidelberg Materials delivered its highest-ever operating profit, demonstrating the effectiveness of strict cost discipline and active price management
- The 6% like-for-like RCO growth on only 1% revenue growth shows significant operational leverage and margin expansion
- The company's 12% EPS CAGR since 2020 ranks among the strongest in the European building materials sector
- The 2026 RCO guidance range of EUR 3.40-3.75B suggests management expects continued growth but acknowledges macro uncertainty
- The ongoing share buyback program (EUR 1.2B total) is meaningfully reducing the share count and boosting per-share metrics
- Heidelberg Materials' pure-play heavy building materials strategy is delivering consistent margin improvement

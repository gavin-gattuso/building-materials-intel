# Installed Building Products Reports Record Q4 and FY2025 Profitability

## Q4 2025 Results
- Net income: increased 14.5% to a record $76.6 million
- Net income per diluted share: increased 18.4% to $2.83

## Acquisition Activity
### 2025 Full Year
- Completed 11 acquisitions representing over $64 million of annual revenue

### Q4 2025 and Early 2026
- Completed acquisitions adding approximately $45 million of annual revenue
- February 2, 2026: completed three acquisitions with combined annual sales over $22 million

### Key Recent Acquisitions
- Thermo-Tech Mechanical Insulation (finalized Feb 2, 2026): ~$13 million in revenue, enhances insulation service capabilities in the Midwest
- Biomax (acquired Jan 19, 2026): ~$5 million in revenue
- CKV (acquired Dec 11, 2025): ~$4 million in revenue
- Northstar Comfort Services: part of the $45 million annual sales addition

### 2026 Acquisition Target
- Expects to acquire at least $100 million of annual revenue in 2026

## Strategic Context
- IBP is the largest U.S. installer of insulation and complementary building products
- Continues aggressive bolt-on acquisition strategy
- Focuses on geographic expansion and service capability enhancement
- Insulation demand supported by building code upgrades and energy efficiency requirements

# JELD-WEN Reports Q4 2025 Results: Revenue Beats Estimates Despite Double-Digit Decline, Sets 2026 Guidance

## Key Details
- Q4 2025 net revenues of $802.0 million, down 10.5% YoY but beat Wall Street estimate of $745.2 million
- Loss per share of $0.47, wider than analyst forecasts
- Adjusted EBITDA of $14.8 million, significantly better than the $7.91 million consensus
- Full-year 2025 net revenues of $3.21 billion, down 14.9%
- Full-year net loss from continuing operations of $620.1 million
- Non-cash goodwill impairments of $334.6 million and $129.2 million valuation expense on US tax attributes
- 2025 Adjusted EBITDA of $120.1 million
- 2026 guidance: Revenue $2.95-$3.10 billion, Adjusted EBITDA $100-$150 million
- Expected 2026 operating cash flow of approximately $40 million
- Stock surged 25.8% on results day due to operational beat

## Analysis
- JELD-WEN's situation is stark: a $620M annual loss and 15% revenue decline reflect the severe downturn in residential construction and renovation markets. However, the stock rally on the Q4 beat suggests the bar was set very low. The 2026 guidance implies continued revenue decline of 5-10%, making JELD-WEN a turnaround story that will require meaningful market recovery to succeed.

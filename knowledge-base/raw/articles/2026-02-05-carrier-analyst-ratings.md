# Carrier Global Analyst Consensus at Buy Despite Price Target Cuts Post Q4

## Key Details
- Consensus rating: Buy from 13 analysts (as of January 16, 2026)
- Average one-year price target: $73.49 (range: $55.55 to $94.50)
- Oppenheimer downgraded from Outperform to Perform (October 2025)
- Wolfe Research maintained Outperform, lowered target from $75 to $74
- RBC Capital maintained Outperform, lowered target from $75 to $70
- Wells Fargo cut price target to $58.00
- Analysts became more pessimistic post-Q4, leading to weaker revenue outlook and minor EPS estimate cuts

## Analysis
- The analyst community remains broadly constructive on Carrier despite Q4 disappointment. The data center growth story is the primary bull case, while residential HVAC weakness and integration of Viessmann are the bear arguments. The wide price target range ($55 to $95) reflects the uncertainty about how fast the data center business can scale relative to ongoing residential headwinds.

# Trane Technologies: Upgrade To Buy On Cheaper Valuation And Better Fundamentals

## Key Points
- Trane Technologies upgraded to Buy rating as valuation resets to 28x forward earnings amid strengthening growth drivers
- 2026 guidance: 6-7% organic revenue growth and adjusted EPS of $14.65 to $14.85, reflecting 12-14% EPS growth
- Reported revenue growth guidance of approximately 8.5-9.5% for 2026
- Commercial HVAC orders surged with applied business orders up approximately 120% year-over-year
- Record backlog of $7.8 billion driven by data center cooling demand
- Data center HVAC demand is a secular growth driver providing multi-year visibility
- Strong margin expansion expected as higher-margin commercial projects flow through

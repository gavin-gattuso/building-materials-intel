# Consumer Confidence Edges Up to 91.8 in March 2026

## Index Values (March 2026)
- Overall: 91.8 (up 0.8 from February's 91.0)
- Present Situation: 123.3 (up 4.6 points)
- Expectations: 70.9 (down 1.7 points)

## GDP Forecasts
- RSM: 2.2% growth in 2026
- Goldman Sachs: 2.8% growth in 2026 (tariff fade + tax cuts)

## Other Indicators
- Recession probability: 30% (down from 40%)
- Consumer spending: focused on "cheap thrills" and necessities
- Inflation expectations: rising due to tariff passthrough and oil prices

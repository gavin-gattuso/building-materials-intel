# Advanced Drainage Systems: Doing Great; Doesn't Mean Gains Deserve To Flow

## Key Points
- Advanced Drainage Systems raised FY26 revenue guidance to $2.945B amid strong Allied and Infiltrator product growth
- Outlined $3.015B revenue target and highlighted NDS integration progress with expected $25M synergies within three years
- Recent $875M net acquisition of National Diversified Sales (NDS) adds $313M in revenue
- Stock outperformed the S&P 500 with a 20.9% gain since September 2025
- Despite strong execution, analyst argues valuation is stretched and gains may not flow proportionally
- Infrastructure spending and stormwater management regulations provide secular demand tailwinds
- Hold rating maintained given premium valuation relative to growth rate

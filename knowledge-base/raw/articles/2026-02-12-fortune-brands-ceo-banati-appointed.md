# Fortune Brands Innovations Appoints Amit Banati as CEO, Succeeding Nicholas Fink

## Key Details
- Board appointed Amit Banati as CEO, effective May 13, 2026
- Nicholas Fink to depart as CEO on April 1, 2026
- Susan Kilsby (Board Chair) to serve as Executive Chair and assume CEO duties during transition
- Banati's background: CFO of Kenvue (since May 2025), prior roles at Kellanova, Mondelez, Cadbury Schweppes, and Procter & Gamble
- Banati served on FBIN Board for nearly 6 years, Chair of Audit Committee since May 2024
- Structured board-led succession plan

## Analysis
- The CEO transition from Fink (who led the company through its separation from Fortune Brands Home & Security and transformation into FBIN) to an outsider with deep consumer products experience suggests the Board is seeking a fresh perspective on operational efficiency and brand management. This was subsequently upended by activist investor pressure (see subsequent article).

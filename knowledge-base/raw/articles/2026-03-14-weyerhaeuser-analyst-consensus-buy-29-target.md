# Weyerhaeuser Holds Buy Consensus with $29-31 Price Target; 19% Upside Implied

## Key Details
- Consensus rating: Buy from 7-9 analysts (depending on source)
- Average price target: $29.00-$31.00 (range: $26.00-$33.00)
- Implied upside: ~18.7% from current levels
- Q1 2026 results due April 30, 2026
- Analysts expect earnings to decline ahead of Q1 2026 report
- Quarterly dividend of $0.21/share ($0.84 annualized); yield ~3.14%
- Presented at Citi's Miami Conference discussing strategic growth path

## Analysis
- The Buy consensus for Weyerhaeuser reflects confidence in the company's diversified business model (timber + real estate + climate solutions) providing downside protection even in a weak lumber market.
- The ~3.1% dividend yield provides income support while investors wait for the lumber cycle to turn, a key advantage of the REIT structure.
- The $26-33 price target range is relatively tight compared to pure lumber producers, reflecting Weyerhaeuser's more predictable cash flow profile.
- The Citi conference presentation on the "strategic growth path" suggests management is actively marketing the 2030 EBITDA growth story to institutional investors.

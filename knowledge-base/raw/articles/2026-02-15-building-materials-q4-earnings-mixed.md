# Building Materials Q4 2025 Earnings: Sector Misses Revenue by 1.2%

## Sector Overview
- Building materials sector underperformed in Q4 2025
- Missed revenue estimates by 1.2% on average
- Average share prices down 10.2% since earnings results
- Mixed results amid economic pressures

## Company-by-Company Results (February 2026 Reporting Period)

### Martin Marietta Materials (Feb 11)
- Record revenue: $6.15 billion (up 9% YoY)
- Adjusted EBITDA margins: ~35.5%
- Aggregate pricing: +12%
- Record gross profit per ton: $8.59

### Vulcan Materials (Feb 17)
- Adjusted EBITDA: $2.3 billion (up 13% YoY) -- record
- Adjusted EBITDA margin: 29.3% (expanded 160 bps)
- Q4 EPS: $1.70 (missed estimate of $2.12)
- 2026 EBITDA guidance: $2.4-$2.6 billion

### Builders FirstSource (Feb 17)
- Q4 sales: $3.36 billion (down 12.1% YoY, missed by 2.8%)
- Q4 EPS: $1.12 (missed estimate of $1.24)
- FY2025 sales: $15.19 billion (down 7.4%)
- 2026 guidance: $14.8-$15.8 billion sales

### Armstrong World (Feb reporting)
- Revenue: $388.3 million (up 5.6% YoY)
- Fell short of analyst expectations by 3%

### Carlisle (Feb reporting)
- Revenue: $1.13 billion (flat YoY)
- Outperformed expectations by 1.4%
- Strong beat on adjusted operating income

### Installed Building Products (Feb 26)
- Record Q4 net income: $76.6 million (up 14.5%)
- EPS: $2.83 (up 18.4%)
- 11 acquisitions in 2025 adding $64M+ annual revenue

### Home Depot (Feb 24-25)
- Q4 revenue: $38.2 billion (down ~3.8% YoY)
- Beat expectations; comps +0.4%

### Lowe's (Feb 25)
- Q4 revenue: ~$20.58 billion (up ~11% YoY)
- Adjusted EPS: $1.98 (up 2.6%)
- Comps: +1.3%

## Key Takeaways
- Aggregates producers (VMC, MLM) showing pricing power despite volume headwinds
- Building products distributors (BLDR) pressured by residential weakness
- Home improvement retail (HD, LOW) stable but cautious
- Insulation/installation (IBP) demonstrating growth through acquisitions
- Infrastructure-exposed companies outperforming residential-exposed ones

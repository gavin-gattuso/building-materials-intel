# Home Depot/SRS Distribution Complete $5.5 Billion GMS Acquisition -- Pro Market Transformation

## Deal Summary

- **Acquirer**: The Home Depot via subsidiary SRS Distribution
- **Target**: GMS Inc.
- **Enterprise Value**: **$5.5 billion** (including net debt)
- **Completed**: September 2025 (earlier than expected)
- **Announced**: June 30, 2025

## GMS Business Profile

- One of the leading distributors of specialty building products
- Products: drywall, ceilings, steel framing, and complementary products
- Serves both residential and commercial end markets in remodeling and construction
- Complements SRS Distribution's existing roofing, landscaping, and exterior building products

## Strategic Rationale

- Further enhances SRS's position as leading multi-category building materials distributor
- Brings differentiated capabilities, product categories, and customer relationships
- SRS + GMS provide unique value proposition to serve residential and commercial Pro customers more holistically
- More fulfillment and service options enabling Pros to complete projects faster and more seamlessly

## Home Depot's Pro Strategy Timeline

| Year | Move | Value |
|------|------|-------|
| 2024 | Acquired SRS Distribution | ~$18.25 billion |
| 2025 | Acquired GMS via SRS | $5.5 billion |
| Combined | Total pro-market investment | ~$23.75 billion |

## Market Impact

- Home Depot and Lowe's combined pro-market acquisitions exceed **$30 billion** in ~18 months
- Fundamental reshaping of building products distribution landscape
- Traditional independent distributors face intensified competitive pressure
- Specialty distribution consolidation accelerating

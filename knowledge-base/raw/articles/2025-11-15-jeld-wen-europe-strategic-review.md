# JELD-WEN Announces Strategic Review of its Europe Business

## Key Details
- Comprehensive review of strategic alternatives for its Europe business initiated in November 2025
- Europe business accounted for $1.1 billion, approximately 28% of global revenue in 2024
- Review aims to optimize portfolio and align resources for sustainable performance
- European markets expected to be broadly flat year-over-year in 2026, with signs of stabilization but no material improvement
- North American windows and doors market expected down low- to mid-single digits in 2026
- New single-family construction expected to see low-single-digit declines
- Repair and remodel expected mid-single-digit declines
- Canada expected to decline at high-single-digit rate

## Analysis
- A potential sale of the Europe business would dramatically reshape JELD-WEN into a primarily North American company. Given that the European business represents nearly a third of revenue and European markets have been weak, a divestiture could simplify the company and generate cash to reduce debt. The market outlook for both North America and Europe remains challenging, with no near-term catalysts for recovery.

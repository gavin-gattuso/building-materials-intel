# Owens Corning Outlines $800M CapEx for 2026 Amid Strategic Shift and Market Headwinds

## Key Points
- Owens Corning reported Q4 2025 earnings and revenue miss along with impairment charges
- Company outlined $800M CapEx plan for 2026 amid strategic repositioning
- OC stock slipped after Q4 miss but management sees business conditions improving in 2026
- Despite lackluster growth, OC maintains solid margins and manageable leverage
- Dividend increased by 15%, signaling confidence in capital allocation and cash flow generation
- 2025 saw progressively more challenging market conditions with weakening U.S. residential trends and distribution destocking in H2
- Strategic shift underway as company integrates Masonite acquisition and optimizes portfolio

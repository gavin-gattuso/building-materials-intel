# Spring 2026 Housing: Hesitant Buyers Despite Softer Pricing

## New Home Sales
- January 2026: 713,104 (SAAR, Zonda metric)
- Down 7.2% from a year ago
- 2026 forecast: ~1% increase in single-family building

## Builder Confidence (NAHB HMI)
- March 2026: 38 (up 1 point from February)
- 70% of builders describe conditions as weaker than expected
- Sales require more effort, tighter margins, more incentives

## Market Dynamics
- Defined by hesitant buyers, not weak demand
- Households delaying due to economic uncertainty
- Pent-up demand expected to re-enter as rates ease

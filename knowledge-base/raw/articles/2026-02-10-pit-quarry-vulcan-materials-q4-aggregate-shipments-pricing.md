# Aggregate Shipments, Pricing Up in Q4 at Vulcan Materials

## Key Points
- Vulcan Materials reported increases in both aggregate shipments and pricing in Q4
- Company expects to deliver between $2.4 and $2.6 billion of adjusted EBITDA in 2026
- Continued strength in public construction activity expected to benefit results
- Improving private nonresidential opportunities providing additional upside
- Pricing discipline remains core to Vulcan's strategy across all markets
- Vulcan ranks as the nation's largest crushed stone producer
- Infrastructure demand and megaprojects supporting volume outlook

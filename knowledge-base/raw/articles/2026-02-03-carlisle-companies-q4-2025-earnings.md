# Carlisle Companies Q4 2025: Beats EPS by $0.27; Provides 2026 Guidance

## Key Details
- **Q4 2025 Revenue**: $1.13 billion (up 0.4% YoY, beat estimate of $1.11 billion)
- **Q4 2025 Diluted EPS**: $3.19
- **Q4 2025 Adjusted EPS**: $3.90 (beat estimate of $3.60 by $0.30)
- **CCM Revenue**: $827 million (down 0.8%; -1% organic)
  - Healthy re-roof activity offset by commercial new construction softness
  - Adjusted EBITDA: $222 million; EBITDA margin: 26.8%
- **CWT Revenue**: $301 million (up 4%; -7% organic)
  - M&A contributions offset residential and non-residential new construction softness
  - Adjusted EBITDA: $48 million; EBITDA margin: 15.9%
- **Full-Year 2025 Revenue**: $5.0 billion
- **Full-Year 2025 Adjusted EPS**: $19.40
- **Full-Year 2025 Operating Cash Flow**: $1.1 billion
- **Full-Year 2025 Free Cash Flow**: $972 million
- **2026 Guidance**:
  - Revenue growth: Low single digits (Q1 down low-single-digits, Q2 flat, H2 stronger)
  - Adjusted EBITDA margin expansion: ~50 bps vs. 2025
  - Share repurchase plan: $1.0 billion
  - Expected ROIC: ~25%
  - Free cash flow margin: >15%

## Analysis
- Carlisle's EPS beat demonstrates the company's pricing power and operational discipline in its roofing and building envelope businesses
- CCM's 26.8% EBITDA margin remains industry-leading despite softness in commercial new construction
- The $1.0 billion share repurchase plan for 2026 signals management confidence in cash generation and reflects discipline on M&A valuations
- Vision 2030 strategy continues to drive premium margins through the Carlisle Operating System (COS) and innovation
- The first-half weakness/second-half recovery guidance aligns with broader seasonal patterns and the housing cycle

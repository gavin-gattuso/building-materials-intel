# Construction Labor Demand Drops to 350,000 New Workers Needed in 2026

## Key Statistics
- New workers needed in 2026: 349,000 (some estimates: 500,000)
- 2027 projection: 456,000 new workers needed
- Wages: +4% year-over-year; some firms raising 20%+ to compete
- Recommended salary increase budget: 4.0%

## Workforce Challenges
- 1 in 5 construction workers is 55+
- 71.7% of contractors increased headcount in 2025
- 46% reported zero net workforce growth due to turnover
- 28% of firms affected by immigration enforcement in past 6 months

## Economic Impact
- Skilled labor shortage costs home building sector $10.8B/year
- Estimated 19,000 homes not built due to labor shortages

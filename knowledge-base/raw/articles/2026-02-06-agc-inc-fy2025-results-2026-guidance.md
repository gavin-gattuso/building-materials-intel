# AGC Inc Reports FY2025 Results: Operating Profit Up Despite Flat Sales; Forecasts Growth in FY2026

## Key Details
- FY2025 net sales of JPY 2,058.8 billion (down JPY 8.8B YoY)
- Operating profit improved to JPY 127.5 billion (up JPY 1.6B YoY)
- ROE of 4.7% in FY2025
- Q4 2025 revenue of JPY 546.69 billion, beating forecast by 0.14%
- FY2026 forecast: net sales of JPY 2.2 trillion (up JPY 141.2B), operating profit of JPY 150 billion (up JPY 22.5B)
- FY2026 ROE target: 5%; FY2027 target: 8%+
- Dividend maintained at JPY 210 per share for FY2025
- CEO Yoshinori Hirai stated the company has "hit the bottom"
- Chemical segment growth expected from 2026; life science recovery by 2027
- Automotive glass and LCD glass substrates drove improved margins
- Display glass revenue hit a record JPY 270 billion in Q3 2025

## Analysis
- AGC's stabilization and nascent recovery reflect the broader trend of glass manufacturers benefiting from automotive and display technology demand even as architectural glass markets remain soft.
- The JPY 150B operating profit target for FY2026 represents 17.6% growth, a meaningful acceleration that depends heavily on the chemical segment turnaround.
- CEO Hirai's "hit the bottom" statement is a notable call that signals confidence in the recovery path, but execution risk remains given the uncertain macro environment.
- The low ROE (4.7%) relative to global peers highlights the ongoing need for AGC to improve capital efficiency through restructuring and portfolio optimization.

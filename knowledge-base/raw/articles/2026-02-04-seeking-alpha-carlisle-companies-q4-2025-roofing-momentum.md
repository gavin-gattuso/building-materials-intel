# Carlisle Companies: Residential Construction Driven Momentum

## Key Points
- Carlisle well-positioned to benefit from projected rebound in U.S. residential construction driven by lower interest and mortgage rates in 2026
- 74% of revenue from construction materials providing strong exposure to building recovery cycle
- Company targets $40 adjusted EPS by 2030, supported by aging roof replacement cycles, regulatory tailwinds, and opportunistic M&A
- Strong balance sheet with 1.4x net debt/EBITDA provides capacity for growth investments
- Trading at forward P/E of 17.1x, below its 10-year average, suggesting attractive entry point
- Capital returns include $700M in share buybacks and a 17.6% dividend hike signaling management confidence
- Q4 2025 earnings call presentation published February 4, 2026

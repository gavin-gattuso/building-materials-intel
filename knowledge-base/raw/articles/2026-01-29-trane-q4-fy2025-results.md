# Trane Technologies Reports Strong FY2025 Results: Revenue Up 7%, Record $7.8B Backlog, Raises 2026 Guidance

## Key Details
- Full-year 2025 reported revenues of $21.3 billion, up 7% (organic +6%)
- GAAP continuing EPS of $13.14; adjusted continuing EPS of $13.06, up 16%
- Adjusted EBITDA margin of 20.1%, up 70 basis points
- Free cash flow conversion of 98%
- Q4 2025 bookings of $5.8 billion (organic +22%)
- Full-year 2025 bookings of $22.6 billion (organic +11%)
- Record backlog of $7.8 billion
- 2026 guidance: revenue growth of 8.5-9.5%, organic revenue growth of 6-7%
- 2026 GAAP/adjusted EPS guidance: $14.65-$14.85
- 12% dividend increase to $1.05/share quarterly ($4.20 annualized), payable March 31, 2026

## Analysis
- Trane Technologies' results are exceptional across every metric. The 20%+ EBITDA margin, 98% cash conversion, and record backlog demonstrate best-in-class execution in HVAC. The 22% organic bookings growth in Q4 provides strong visibility into 2026. The 12% dividend increase signals confidence in sustained cash generation. Trane is consistently delivering the most complete financial profile in building materials: strong growth, expanding margins, and robust cash flow. The $7.8B backlog provides 4+ months of revenue visibility.

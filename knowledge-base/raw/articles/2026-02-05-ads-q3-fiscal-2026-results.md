# Advanced Drainage Systems Reports Q3 FY2026 Results, Increases Stock Repurchase Authorization

## Key Details
- Q3 FY2026 net sales of $693.4 million, up 0.4% ($2.8 million increase)
- Net income of $94.0 million, up 14.3% ($11.7 million increase)
- Net income per diluted share of $1.19, up 14.4%
- Adjusted EBITDA of $209.2 million, up 9.3% ($17.7 million increase)
- Year-to-date net sales of $2.4 billion, up 3.7% ($85.1 million)
- Year-to-date net income of $394.6 million, up 5.0%
- Year-to-date EPS of $5.02, up 5.2%
- Updated FY2026 guidance: revenue midpoint $3.015 billion, Adjusted EBITDA midpoint $945 million
- Adjusted EBITDA margin guidance of 31.1-31.6%, representing 50-100 bps improvement vs. prior year
- Increased stock repurchase authorization

## Analysis
- ADS continues to demonstrate resilient performance with expanding margins in the drainage infrastructure space. The 31%+ EBITDA margin is exceptional for building materials and reflects the company's leading position in HDPE pipe and stormwater management. The modest revenue growth but strong profit expansion highlights pricing power and operational leverage. Infrastructure spending and water management requirements remain secular tailwinds for the business.

# ArcelorMittal: Outperformance Confirmed 2025, 2026E Is Coming (Rating Downgrade)

## Key Points
- ArcelorMittal downgraded after significant outperformance versus the broader index in 2025
- Long-term investment thesis remains intact underpinned by robust operational execution and strategic positioning
- Fair value range estimated between $37.50 and $44.00 per share
- Geopolitical tensions, tariffs, and China's steel overcapacity remain key risk factors
- Benefits from regionalization trends and European infrastructure demand growth
- ArcelorMittal warned that delays in resolving global trade disruptions due to tariffs could affect steel consumption forecasts
- Despite uncertainties, shares appear undervalued on a fundamental basis at current levels

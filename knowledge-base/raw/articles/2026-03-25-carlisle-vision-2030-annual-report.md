# Carlisle Companies: Vision 2030 Execution Update and 2025 Annual Report

## Key Details
- **2025 Annual Report Published**: March 25, 2026
- **Vision 2030 Four Pillars**:
  1. Product innovation to drive differentiation
  2. Operational excellence via Carlisle Operating System (COS)
  3. Exceptional customer service through the Carlisle Experience
  4. Strategic M&A to enhance capabilities
- **Recent Bolt-On Acquisitions**: MTL Holdings (May 2024), Plasti-Fab ($259.5M, Dec 2024), Thermafoam, Bonded Logic
- **Plasti-Fab Details**: Canadian EPS insulation; 8 manufacturing locations in Canada, 3 in U.S.; expected +$0.30 adjusted EPS in 2025; $14 million annual synergies within 3 years
- **M&A Discipline**: Elevated valuation expectations making acquisitions more challenging; company leaned into buybacks instead
- **2026 Capital Return**: $1.0 billion share repurchase plan
- **Analyst Consensus**: Buy rating from 6 analysts; average PT $390-$410; range $340-$517
- **Key Financial Targets**: ROIC ~25%, FCF margin >15%

## Analysis
- Carlisle's Vision 2030 positions the company as a pure-play building products platform focused on the building envelope (roofing, insulation, waterproofing)
- The discipline to lean into buybacks when M&A valuations are stretched demonstrates capital allocation maturity
- The Plasti-Fab acquisition strengthens CWT's insulation offering in Canada and the U.S. Midwest, expanding the building envelope content per square foot
- Carlisle's premium valuation (Buy consensus with ~$400 PT) reflects market recognition of its consistent margin expansion and capital returns
- The company's pivot from diversified industrial to pure-play building products continues to unlock shareholder value

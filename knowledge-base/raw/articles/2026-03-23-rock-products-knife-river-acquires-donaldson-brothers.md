# Knife River Acquires Donaldson Brothers Ready Mix

## Key Points
- Knife River Corp. acquired the assets of Donaldson Brothers Ready Mix Inc., an aggregates-based ready-mix supplier in western Montana
- Marks Knife River's third acquisition in its Mountain Segment in 2026
- Knife River also acquired assets of Texcrete Operations LLC and TexAgg LLC earlier in 2026
- Texas acquisition included six ready-mix plants and 85 ready-mix trucks serving Brazos and Montgomery counties
- Acquisitions demonstrate accelerating consolidation trend in regional aggregates and ready-mix markets
- Knife River pursuing bolt-on acquisition strategy to expand geographic footprint

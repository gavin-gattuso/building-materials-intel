# Tariffs, Talent and Tech: The New Rules of Roofing Consolidation

## Key Points
- Three forces reshaping roofing industry consolidation: tariffs, talent shortages, and technology
- Tariff impacts adding cost pressure across roofing material supply chains
- Talent shortages accelerating M&A as companies acquire for workforce access
- Technology capabilities becoming key acquisition criteria
- Consolidation trend expected to continue as smaller contractors face mounting cost pressures
- Scale advantages increasingly important for managing tariff exposure and labor costs
- Industry shifting toward fewer, larger, more technologically sophisticated contractors

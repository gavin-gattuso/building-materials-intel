# Carrier CEO: North American Data Center Orders Surged 400% in Q4

## Key Details
- Q4 2025 North American data center orders up 400% year-over-year
- Applied orders more than tripled in Q4
- Data center revenue grew from ~$1 billion in 2025 to guided $1.5 billion in 2026
- Data center orders expected up approximately 50% for full-year 2026
- AI infrastructure buildout driving hyperscaler demand for compute capacity
- Commercial HVAC on track for sixth consecutive year of double-digit growth
- NVIDIA collaboration on cooling designs for AI factories

## Analysis
- The 400% order growth in data center cooling is extraordinary and reflects the massive capital expenditure cycle underway in AI infrastructure. Carrier's early positioning in this market through its QuantumLeap portfolio and NVIDIA partnership gives it a competitive moat. The acceleration from 400% order growth to 50% expected annual growth suggests Carrier is entering a sustained, multi-year demand cycle rather than a one-time spike. This fundamentally changes Carrier's growth profile and justifies premium valuation.

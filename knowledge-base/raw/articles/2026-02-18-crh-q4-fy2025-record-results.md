# CRH Reports Record Q4 and Full-Year 2025 Results

## Key Details
- **Full-year 2025 total revenues**: $37.4 billion, up 5% year-over-year
- **Net income**: $3.8 billion, up 8% YoY
- **Adjusted EBITDA**: $7.7 billion, up 11% YoY
- **Net income margin**: 10.1% (vs. 9.9% in 2024)
- **Adjusted EBITDA margin**: 20.5% (vs. 19.5% in 2024), a 100 bps expansion
- **Capital deployed**: $5.8 billion in growth investments across portfolio
- **Shareholder returns**: $2.2 billion returned through dividends and share buybacks
- **38 acquisitions** completed during 2025, totaling $4.1 billion invested
- **2026 Guidance**: Net income of $3.9B-$4.1B; Adjusted EBITDA of $8.1B-$8.5B
- **2026 Capital expenditures**: $2.8B-$3.0B expected
- Results announced February 18, 2026

## Analysis
- CRH delivered its strongest year on record, driven by favorable end-market demand, disciplined commercial execution, and contributions from acquisitions
- The company continues to benefit from its S&P 500 inclusion, which has broadened its investor base and improved market visibility
- EBITDA margin expansion of 100 bps signals continued pricing momentum and cost discipline
- The 2026 guidance implies further mid-single-digit growth, supported by infrastructure spending (IIJA) and data center demand
- CRH's aggressive capital deployment ($5.8B) shows confidence in the long-term growth trajectory of building materials in North America
- The $8.1B-$8.5B EBITDA guidance for 2026 represents 5-10% growth, suggesting management expects continued favorable conditions

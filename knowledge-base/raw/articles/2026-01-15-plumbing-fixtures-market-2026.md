# Plumbing Fixtures & Fittings Market 2026: $123 Billion Market Driven by Renovation and Smart Technology

## Key Details
- Market size growing from $114.31 billion (2025) to $123.37 billion (2026) at 7.9% CAGR
- Expected to reach $164.78 billion by 2030 at 7.5% CAGR
- Repair & remodel segment led with 70.3% revenue share (2023 data)
- Major players: Masco Corp (Delta, Hansgrohe), LIXIL (GROHE), Geberit, Kohler, TOTO
- Key trends: water-efficient solutions, corrosion-resistant materials, smart plumbing technology
- Design trends: matte black fixtures lead; brushed brass, champagne gold, gunmetal gaining
- Organic materials and earthy tones dominating bathroom design
- Infrastructure modernization and aging plumbing replacement driving demand
- Premium and customizable solutions expanding in both residential and commercial

## Analysis
- The 7.9% growth rate in plumbing fixtures outpaces broader construction growth, reflecting the strong renovation tailwind and premiumization trend. Masco (Delta, Hansgrohe), LIXIL (GROHE), and Geberit are all well-positioned in the premium segment. The 70%+ renovation share means plumbing fixtures are less cyclical than new construction-dependent categories like windows and doors. Smart plumbing technology (leak detection, automated shutoffs) is an emerging growth driver, as evidenced by Fortune Brands' Moen Flo double-digit growth.

# Building Material Price Growth Remains Elevated (3.5% YoY)

## Key Statistics
- Overall: +3.5% YoY (largest since January 2023)
- Price growth above 3.0% since June 2025
- Metal molding and trim: +~50% YoY
- Softwood lumber: below prior year levels
- Ready-mix concrete: softened

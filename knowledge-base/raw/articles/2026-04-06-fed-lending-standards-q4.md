# Fed Survey: Lending Standards Tightened for Commercial Loans in Q4 2025

## C&I Loans
- 5-10% of banks tightened standards
- Stronger demand from large/middle-market firms
- Unchanged demand for small firms
- 10-20% reported lower credit line costs for larger borrowers

## Commercial Real Estate
- Modest easing for multifamily
- Unchanged for construction/land development overall
- Divergence: large banks eased, smaller banks tightened
- Overall CRE demand: stronger

## Residential Mortgages
- Standards basically unchanged
- Demand weaker across most categories
- HELOC demand: modestly stronger

## 2026 Outlook
- Banks expect standards to remain unchanged
- Demand expected to strengthen across all categories
- Banks favoring AI-exposed firms

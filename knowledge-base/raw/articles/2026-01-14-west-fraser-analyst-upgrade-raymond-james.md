# West Fraser Upgraded to Outperform at Raymond James; Mixed Analyst Sentiment

## Key Details
- Raymond James upgraded West Fraser to Outperform from Market Perform on January 14, 2026
- Previous downgrades in late 2025: CIBC downgraded to Neutral from Outperformer (December 2025); RBC lowered price target to $85 from $91
- Consensus rating: Moderate Buy (2 Buy, 1 Hold from 3 analysts)
- Average one-year price target: ~$67-$112 USD depending on source (significant dispersion)
- TSX-listed targets: average CAD $139 (range CAD $123-$157)
- West Fraser set annual shareholder meeting for April 22, 2026

## Analysis
- The Raymond James upgrade at the start of 2026 suggests some analysts see a trough in West Fraser's lumber cycle, but the wide dispersion of price targets reflects deep uncertainty about the recovery timeline.
- The contrast between the CIBC downgrade (December 2025) and Raymond James upgrade (January 2026) illustrates the sharp disagreement among analysts on Canadian lumber producer outlooks.
- The shareholder meeting on April 22 will be closely watched for any strategic announcements, particularly around further capacity rationalization or potential corporate actions.

# Steel Dynamics: Analysts Maintain Buy Despite Q4 Miss; Upbeat 2026 Revisions

## Key Details
- **Analyst Consensus**: Buy (7 Buy, 4 Hold, 0 Sell as of March 2026)
- **Average Price Target**: $182.63 to $194.14 (varies by source)
- **Price Target Range**: $165 (low) to $210 (high)
- **Stock Reaction**: Down 5.3% after Q4 results despite upbeat 2026 earnings revisions
- **Q1 2026 Guidance Impact**: EPS of $2.73-$2.77 (50%+ sequential increase from Q4) boosted analyst confidence
- **Key Catalysts**:
  - Aluminum mill ramp-up (Columbus, MS) to 90% utilization by year-end
  - Higher steel prices from tariff protections
  - Sinton mill operating at improved rates
  - Lean customer inventories supporting demand recovery

## Analysis
- The disconnect between STLD's weak Q4 results and strong analyst outlook reflects the forward-looking nature of the steel cycle turning
- Analysts are pricing in the Q1 2026 step-change in earnings driven by higher steel prices and the aluminum platform contribution
- The aluminum mill achieving 90% utilization by year-end 2026 would represent a major derisking event for the $2.7 billion investment
- STLD's diversification into aluminum sets it apart from Nucor and other pure-play steel producers, creating a differentiated earnings stream
- The 7 Buy/4 Hold/0 Sell consensus is the most bullish among steel stocks, reflecting confidence in STLD's growth investments

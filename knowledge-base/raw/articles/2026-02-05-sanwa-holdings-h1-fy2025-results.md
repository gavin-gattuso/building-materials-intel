# Sanwa Holdings Reports Record Profits in H1 FY2025 Despite Revenue Decline

## Key Details
- H1 FY2025 net sales of JPY 309.2 billion, down 1.5% YoY
- Operating profit rose 4.3% to JPY 33.7 billion
- Profit attributable to owners increased 11.3% to JPY 26.45 billion, exceeding forecasts
- Record-high profits driven by strong Japan business
- North America and Europe weaker due to tariffs, high interest rates, and trade friction
- Full-year FY2025 forecasts: net sales JPY 654.0 billion (-1.3%), operating profit JPY 81.0 billion (+0.6%), profit JPY 58.0 billion (+0.8%)
- EPS forecast of JPY 270.70, operating margin 12.4%
- Asia aiming for local-currency sales growth in Vietnam and Taiwan
- Market recovery in North America and Europe expected from FY2026

## Analysis
- Sanwa Holdings' ability to generate record profits despite falling revenue demonstrates strong cost discipline and favorable product mix in Japan. As parent of Overhead Door Corporation in the US, Sanwa is directly exposed to US construction cycles and tariffs. The anticipated recovery in North America from FY2026 is a positive catalyst, though tariff uncertainty remains a headwind for the door and access solutions market.

# Outlook: Challenges Remain in 2026

## Key Points
- Cement consumption forecast to grow only marginally in 2026, with risks to the downside
- Cement producers face notable challenges including weak demand in key markets
- Global cement market outlook remains cautious amid economic uncertainty
- Regional divergence persists with US and emerging markets offering better prospects than Europe
- Pricing discipline among major producers expected to support margins
- Decarbonization investments continuing despite demand headwinds
- Supply-side constraints providing support to pricing even as volumes remain soft

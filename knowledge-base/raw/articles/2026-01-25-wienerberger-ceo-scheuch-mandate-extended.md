# Wienerberger CEO Heimo Scheuch's Mandate Extended Through 2029; New CFO Dagmar Steinert

## Key Details
- **CEO Extension**: Supervisory Board extended CEO Heimo Scheuch's mandate until AGM 2029
- **Scheuch Tenure**: CEO since August 1, 2009 (17+ years)
- **Age**: 58 years old
- **New CFO**: Dagmar Steinert appointed CFO effective March 1, 2025
- **Transformation Record**: Under Scheuch, Wienerberger transformed from a brick manufacturer to a diversified building material and infrastructure solutions provider
- **Geographic Reach**: Operations across Europe and North America
- **Strategic Direction**: Continued focus on renovation solutions, sustainability, and M&A (Italcer being latest example)

## Analysis
- Scheuch's mandate extension through 2029 provides leadership continuity during a critical period of strategic transformation, including the Italcer integration
- The appointment of Dagmar Steinert as CFO signals potential evolution in financial strategy and capital allocation
- Scheuch's 17-year tenure as CEO is unusually long for a European building materials company and reflects the Board's confidence in his execution of the transformation strategy
- This stability contrasts with leadership changes at other building materials companies and provides investors with continuity of strategic vision

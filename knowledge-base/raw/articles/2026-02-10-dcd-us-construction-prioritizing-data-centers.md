# US Construction Firmly Prioritizing Data Centers as Growth Projected Following a Year of Decline

## Key Points
- Investment in structures projected to pivot to modest growth of 1.8% in 2026
- Growth driven in part by AI-related data center spending
- Data center construction is top priority for US construction sector
- Overall construction sector recovering from a year of decline
- Data center demand creating competition for construction labor and materials
- Steel and concrete demand from data centers growing but still less than 2% of total cement consumption nationally
- Construction materials tariffs (steel/aluminum at 50%, effective rate for construction goods 25-30%) adding cost pressure

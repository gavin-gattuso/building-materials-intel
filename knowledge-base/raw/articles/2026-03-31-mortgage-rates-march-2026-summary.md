# Mortgage Rates Summary: March 2026 -- Rates Increase Amid Economic Uncertainty

## 30-Year Fixed Mortgage Rate

- **March 20, 2026**: 6.25%
- **March 24, 2026**: 6.43%
- **March 31, 2026**: 6.37% (Zillow average)
- Rates increased by approximately **half a percentage point** during March

## 15-Year Fixed Mortgage Rate

- **March 31, 2026**: 5.75%

## Refinance Rates

- 30-year refinance: **6.72%** as of March 31, 2026

## Key Drivers

- Federal Reserve held rates steady at 3.50%-3.75% on March 18
- Unemployment rose during the month
- Inflation progress stalled
- Iran/Strait of Hormuz crisis adding uncertainty and inflationary pressure
- Oil price shock threatening to keep inflation above 2% target

## Housing Market Impact

- Affordable mortgage rate options for buyers remain scarce
- Elevated rates continue to suppress existing home sales (lock-in effect)
- Builder incentives remain high: 64% of builders offering incentives
- 37% of builders cutting prices, average reduction of 6%
- Housing affordability remains the dominant constraint on market activity

## Forecast Context

- Fed dot plot projects only one rate cut in 2026
- Most forecasts anticipate moderation rather than sharp improvement
- Gradual improvement expected but not a return to pre-2022 rate levels

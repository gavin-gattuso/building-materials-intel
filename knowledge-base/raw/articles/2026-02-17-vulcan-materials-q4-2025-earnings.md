# Vulcan Materials Q4 2025 Earnings: Revenue Miss Despite Strong Full-Year Growth

## Q4 2025 Results (Reported February 17, 2026)

- **Revenue**: $1.91 billion (missed forecast of $1.95 billion)
- **EPS**: $1.70 (missed expected $2.13)
- Stock declined on the miss

## Full Year 2025 Highlights

- **Adjusted EBITDA**: $2.3 billion, reflecting **13% growth**
- **EBITDA Margin**: 29.3%, expansion of **160 basis points**
- **Aggregate Cash Gross Profit Per Ton**: $11.33, up **7%** -- reached management's long-term target range of $11-$12
- **Aggregate Shipments**: approximately 227 million tons, up **3%**; same-store shipments slightly lower; growth was acquisition-driven
- **Aggregates Mix-Adjusted Pricing**: increased **6%** for full year; **5%** uptick in Q4
- **Operating Cash Flow**: over $1.8 billion, up **29%**
- **Free Cash Flow**: rose over **40%**

## 2026 Guidance

- Aggregate shipments expected to increase **1-3%**
- Freight-adjusted average selling prices to rise **4-6%**
- Adjusted EBITDA projected between **$2.4 billion and $2.6 billion**

## Market Context

- Vulcan Materials is the largest US producer of construction aggregates (crushed stone, sand, gravel)
- Infrastructure spending via IIJA supporting demand
- Data center and manufacturing construction providing tailwinds
- Residential slowdown creating partial headwinds

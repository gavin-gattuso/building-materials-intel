# Canfor Upgraded to Strong Buy at Raymond James; Price Targets Trimmed Elsewhere

## Key Details
- Raymond James upgraded Canfor to Strong Buy from Outperform; designated as "Analyst Current Favorite"
- CIBC lowered price target to CAD $15 from CAD $16
- TD Securities lowered target to CAD $17 from CAD $18
- Scotiabank lowered target to CAD $13 from CAD $14
- Consensus: Moderate Buy (2 Buy, 0 Hold, 1 Sell)
- Average price target: CAD $15.00 (range: CAD $13.00 - CAD $17.00)

## Analysis
- The Raymond James Strong Buy rating and "Analyst Current Favorite" designation stands out against the backdrop of universal price target cuts, suggesting a deep-value contrarian thesis.
- The wide analyst disagreement (one Strong Buy vs. one Sell) reflects the extreme uncertainty around Canadian lumber producers' earnings recovery timing.
- At current depressed levels, Canfor's valuation reflects a worst-case scenario for lumber markets; the Strong Buy case likely rests on eventual tariff relief and lumber price recovery.
- The price target range of CAD $13-17 is remarkably tight, suggesting limited upside consensus even among bulls.

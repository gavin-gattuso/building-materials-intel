# ASSA ABLOY Q4 2025 Earnings Call Presentation

## Key Points
- ASSA ABLOY published Q4 2025 earnings call presentation on February 5, 2026
- Earnings estimates stable at $0.69 per share for 2025 and $0.86 per share for 2026
- Revenue estimates for 2026 at approximately $16.67 billion
- Average analyst target price of approximately $16 per ADR share
- Company continues to execute on digital access solutions strategy including smart locks and mobile credentials
- Geographic diversification across Americas, EMEIA, Asia Pacific, and Global Technologies divisions
- M&A pipeline remains active as ASSA ABLOY continues bolt-on acquisition strategy in security and access

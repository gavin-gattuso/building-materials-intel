# ArcelorMittal Q4 2025: EPS Beats by 39% Despite Revenue Miss

## Key Details
- **Q4 2025 EPS**: $0.86, beating analyst estimate of $0.62 by 38.71%
- **Q4 2025 Revenue**: $14.97 billion, missing expectations of $15.58 billion
- **Full-Year 2025 EBITDA**: $6.54 billion ($121 per ton)
- **Full-Year 2025 Net Income**: $3.15 billion
- **Full-Year 2025 Basic EPS**: $4.13
- **Investable Cash Flow**: $1.9 billion for FY 2025
- **Year-End Net Debt**: $7.9 billion
- **Total Liquidity**: $11.0 billion
- **Strategic Capex**: $1.1 billion invested in renewables, EAF capacity, and electrical steels
- Earnings release date: February 5, 2026
- Q1 2026 earnings scheduled for April 30, 2026

## Analysis
- ArcelorMittal delivered resilient profitability despite lower revenue, driven by disciplined cost management and strategic growth investments
- The EPS beat reflects operational efficiency improvements and contributions from the Calvert EAF ramp-up
- Approximately 40% of EBITDA generated in North America, underscoring the strategic importance of the region
- The company continues to pivot toward higher-value products (electrical steels, automotive) and lower-carbon production (EAF)
- Strong liquidity position ($11B) provides flexibility for continued strategic investments and capital returns

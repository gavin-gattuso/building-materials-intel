# Lowe's $8.8 Billion Foundation Building Materials Integration: Reshaping Pro Distribution

## Deal Summary

- **Acquirer**: Lowe's Companies
- **Target**: Foundation Building Materials (FBM)
- **Value**: **$8.8 billion**
- **Closed**: October 9, 2025
- **Announced**: August 20, 2025

## FBM Business Profile

- Over **370 locations** across the United States and Canada
- Leading North American distributor of interior building products
- Products include: drywall, metal framing, ceiling systems, commercial doors and hardware, insulation, and complementary products
- Serves large residential and commercial pros in construction and repair/remodel

## Strategic Rationale

- Accelerates Lowe's "Total Home" strategy targeting large Pro customers
- **$250 billion** total addressable market for professional customers
- Enhanced offering through expanded product assortment, faster fulfillment, improved digital tools, and trade credit platform

## Integration with Artisan Design Group

- Creates cross-selling opportunities between FBM, Lowe's, and recently acquired Artisan Design Group
- Greater vertical integration from distribution/logistics to on-site installation
- Stronger foundation to serve contractors across every stage of a project

## Competitive Context

- Follows Home Depot's acquisition of SRS Distribution (2024) and GMS ($5.5B, 2025)
- Both big-box retailers aggressively pursuing the professional contractor market
- Pro channel becoming the key competitive battleground in building products distribution
- Combined moves represent over **$20 billion** in pro-market acquisitions in 18 months

# Roofing Suppliers Shine at IRE 2026

## Key Points
- Carlisle SynTec Systems unveiled ThermaThin 7, a thinner, higher-performing insulation product
- ThermaThin 7 designed to cut truckloads and speed installation time
- Product launch part of Carlisle's Vision 2030 Strategy
- ThermaThin 7 expected to be available before peak summer roofing season
- Owens Corning framed technology as core part of contractor support strategy
- GAF highlighted resilient and sustainable roofing solutions at IRE 2026
- Major suppliers showcasing innovation focused on efficiency, sustainability, and labor productivity

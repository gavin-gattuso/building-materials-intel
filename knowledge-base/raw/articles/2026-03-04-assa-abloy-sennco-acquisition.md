# ASSA ABLOY Acquires Sennco Solutions and Sam's Garage Doors

## Key Details
- Acquired Sennco Solutions (US) in March 2026, a provider of asset protection technology for retail security (primarily health and beauty segment)
- Sennco had 2025 sales of approximately $33 million with a strong EBIT margin
- Also completed acquisition of Sam's Garage Doors on March 4, 2026
- ASSA ABLOY has completed almost 400 acquisitions since 1994
- Company targets 5% acquired growth over a business cycle
- Peak acquisition activity in 2025 with 19 acquisitions

## Analysis
- These acquisitions continue ASSA ABLOY's prolific bolt-on M&A strategy. Sennco expands the company's retail security portfolio, a growing niche as retailers invest in anti-theft technology. The pace of acquisitions (19 in 2025) demonstrates the company's disciplined M&A machine and its ability to continuously build scale in fragmented access and security markets.

# What Roofing Contractors Will Face in 2026

## Key Points
- Contractors navigating labor constraints that show little sign of easing
- Heightened sensitivity to political outcomes that may directly impact workforce availability and regulatory enforcement
- Tightening backlogs signaling potential demand softening
- Renewed focus on collections as margins narrow
- 2025 moved roofing industry out of volume-driven environment into one rewarding operational discipline
- Demand uneven by market and sector, forcing closer attention to labor, backlog quality, and cash flow
- Contractors identified customization (40%), expansion into new trades (36%), and digital transformation (34%) as biggest growth opportunities

# CEMEX Hosts 2026 Analyst Day: Unveils Sprint Strategy and Boosts Shareholder Returns

## Key Details
- **Event date**: February 26, 2026
- **Business plan**: Project Cutting Edge Sprint (2025-2027)
- **Growth targets**: Annual sales growth of ~5%, EBITDA growth of ~10% in 2026-2027
- **Cost savings**: Incremental $125 million savings expected in 2026; cumulative $400 million by 2027
- **Savings breakdown**: $200M from operating efficiencies + $200M from overhead reductions
- **ROIC expansion**: Expected +170 bps vs. 2025
- **FCF/EBITDA conversion**: Targeting 47% by 2027, approaching 50% goal
- **Dividend**: Proposed cash dividend of US$180 million (up ~40% YoY)
- **Share buyback**: Up to US$500 million over three years
- **Shareholder distribution target**: 40-50% of available free cash flow by 2030
- **Acquisition announced**: Omega Products International (leading stucco manufacturer in western U.S., ~$23M EBITDA/year)
- **Appointment**: Will Price named SVP of M&A for Cemex US (November 2025)

## Analysis
- CEMEX is accelerating its transformation from a commodity cement company to a value-added building solutions provider
- The Sprint strategy's 10% annual EBITDA growth target signals confidence in both organic and M&A-driven expansion
- The 40% dividend increase and $500M buyback program mark a major shift toward shareholder returns after years of deleveraging
- The Omega Products acquisition extends CEMEX's U.S. adjacency strategy into stucco, adding a higher-margin product line
- Appointing a dedicated SVP of M&A for the U.S. business underscores the company's intent to aggressively pursue bolt-on deals

# BFS CEO Jackson Maps a Bigger Role in Homebuilding Workflows

## Key Points
- Builders FirstSource CEO Peter Jackson positioning company as full-service partner for homebuilders
- Strategy centered on acquisitions, installation services, and the myBLDR digital portal
- Company acquired truss, wall panel, and millwork operations to expand value-added manufacturing
- Also acquired Pleasant Valley Homes, expanding into modular/offsite construction
- Digital transformation through myBLDR portal streamlining builder procurement workflows
- BFS expanding offsite/modular building capabilities despite scalability hurdles in the sector
- Company leveraging scale to offer broader bill of materials sourcing to large builders

# Saint-Gobain Launches "Lead & Grow" Strategic Plan: EUR 20B Capital Deployment Through 2030

## Key Details
- New strategic plan "Lead & Grow" announced for 2026-2030 period, succeeding "Grow & Impact"
- EUR 20 billion total capital allocation planned over 5 years
- EUR 12 billion earmarked for growth investments and acquisitions (net of divestments)
- EUR 8 billion for shareholder returns (EUR 6B dividends + EUR 2B share buybacks)
- Financial targets: mid-single-digit local-currency sales growth, EBITDA margin 15-18%, free cash flow conversion >50%, ROCE >13%
- Portfolio rotation to exceed 20% of sales by 2030
- North America and Asia targeted to reach 60% of sales long-term, up from ~50% currently
- Construction chemicals sales targeted at EUR 9 billion by 2030 vs. EUR 6.5 billion in 2024
- Net debt/EBITDA target: 1.5x-2.0x

## Analysis
- The EUR 20B capital allocation plan is one of the largest in the building materials industry and signals Saint-Gobain's intent to become the undisputed global leader in light-side construction.
- The geographic shift toward North America and Asia reflects a strategic pivot away from Europe's stagnating construction markets toward higher-growth regions.
- The 15-18% EBITDA margin band provides room for improvement from the current 15.5%, but sets a floor that demonstrates management's confidence in the transformed portfolio.
- For competitors like Owens Corning and AGC, Saint-Gobain's aggressive M&A posture raises the stakes in construction chemicals and specialty building materials.

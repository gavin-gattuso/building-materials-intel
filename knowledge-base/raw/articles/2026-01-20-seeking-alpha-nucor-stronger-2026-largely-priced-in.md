# Nucor: A Stronger 2026, Largely Priced In (Downgrade)

## Key Points
- Nucor downgraded on Seeking Alpha despite expectations for higher earnings in 2026
- Company expects stronger 2026 earnings citing strong demand, positive seasonal trends, fewer outages, and record backlogs
- Nucor plans to reduce CapEx from $3.4 billion to around $2.5 billion in 2026, supporting higher free cash flow
- $10B capex cycle nearing completion should boost earnings from 2026, with order backlog up 30% year-over-year
- Nucor targets 5% increase in steel mill shipments for 2026 while advancing major growth projects
- Stock trades at a modest 12x-13x earnings multiple with minimal net debt, but analyst argues upside is already priced in
- Q1 2026 EPS guidance in the range of $2.70 to $2.80 per diluted share

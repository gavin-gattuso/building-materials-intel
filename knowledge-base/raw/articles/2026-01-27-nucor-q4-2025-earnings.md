# Nucor Q4 2025: Earnings Miss Estimates but Strong Q1 2026 Guidance

## Key Details
- **Q4 2025 Net Earnings**: $378 million, or $1.64 per diluted share
- **Q4 2025 Adjusted Net Earnings**: $400 million, or $1.73 per diluted share (excluded impairment charges)
- **Q4 2025 Net Sales**: $7.69 billion (missed estimate of $7.87 billion)
- **Analyst EPS Miss**: Adjusted EPS of $1.73 vs. $1.86 estimate
- **Q3 2025 Comparison**: Net earnings of $607 million ($2.63/share)
- **Q4 2024 Comparison**: Net earnings of $287 million ($1.22/share)
- **Q1 2026 Guidance**: EPS expected in range of $2.70 to $2.80 per diluted share
- **Q1 2026 Outlook**: Earnings expected to increase across all three operating segments vs. Q4 2025
- **Dividend**: Increased to $0.56/quarter, marking 53 consecutive years of dividend increases (211th consecutive quarterly payment)
- **Capital Expenditure Plan**: Approximately $2.5 billion in 2026 ($800 million for maintenance)

## Analysis
- While Q4 missed estimates, the strong Q1 2026 guidance ($2.70-$2.80) demonstrates improving fundamentals driven by higher steel prices and tariff protections
- The 53-year consecutive dividend increase streak underscores Nucor's disciplined capital allocation and through-cycle resilience
- Steel mills segment expected to show the largest Q1 improvement due to higher average selling prices and volumes
- Nucor's domestic-only production model positions it as a primary beneficiary of 50% Section 232 tariffs

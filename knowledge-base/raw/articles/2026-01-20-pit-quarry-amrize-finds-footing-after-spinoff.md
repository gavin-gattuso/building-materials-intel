# Amrize Finds Footing After Spinoff, Looks Ahead to 2026

## Key Points
- Amrize (spun off from Holcim) establishing itself as independent entity in the North American market
- Company looking ahead to 2026 with focus on aggregates and building materials growth
- Amrize entered agreement to acquire PB Materials Holdings Inc., an aggregates business in West Texas
- Acquisition adds more than 50 years of aggregates reserves plus complementary ready-mix concrete network
- Spinoff represents Holcim's strategic shift to become more Europe-weighted
- Amrize positioning for infrastructure-driven demand in US market
- Independent structure expected to provide greater operational and capital allocation flexibility

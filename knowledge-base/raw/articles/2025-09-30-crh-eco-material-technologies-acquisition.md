# CRH Completes $2.1 Billion Acquisition of Eco Material Technologies

## Key Details
- **Deal value**: $2.1 billion
- **Target**: Eco Material Technologies, North America's leading supplier of Supplementary Cementitious Materials (SCMs)
- **Agreement announced**: July 29, 2025
- **Completion**: Q3 2025
- **Funding**: Cash on hand; no expected change in credit ratings
- Eco Material Technologies is a leading supplier of fly ash and natural pozzolans used as cement substitutes
- CRH completed 38 total acquisitions in 2025 for $4.1 billion

## Analysis
- This acquisition positions CRH at the forefront of the transition to next-generation cement and concrete
- SCMs are increasingly critical as the cement industry pursues decarbonization; fly ash and pozzolans reduce the carbon footprint of concrete by partially replacing Portland cement
- The deal strengthens CRH's position as a leading cementitious player in North America, expanding its national distribution network
- With infrastructure projects increasingly requiring lower-carbon concrete mixes, this acquisition gives CRH a strategic advantage in winning sustainability-focused contracts
- The $2.1B price tag makes it one of CRH's largest single acquisitions, reflecting the strategic importance of SCMs in the building materials value chain

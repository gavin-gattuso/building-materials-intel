# UBS Upgrades Nucor, Steel Dynamics on Tariff Support and Higher Price Outlook

## Key Points
- UBS upgraded both Nucor (NUE) and Steel Dynamics (STLD) to Buy ratings
- UBS maintained a Neutral rating on Commercial Metals Company for comparison
- Upgrades driven by increased U.S. steel trade protections and higher price forecasts
- UBS raised price target for Nucor to $160
- UBS raised price target for Steel Dynamics to $149
- Tariff environment expected to support domestic steel pricing and margins through 2026
- Both companies positioned as primary beneficiaries of protectionist trade policies

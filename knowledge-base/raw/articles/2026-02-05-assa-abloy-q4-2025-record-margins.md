# ASSA ABLOY Reports Q4 2025 Results with Record EBIT Margin of 16.8%

## Key Details
- Q4 2025 total sales of MSEK 38,307, down 3% reported but organic sales grew 4%
- 10% negative impact from foreign exchange offset strong underlying performance
- Record EBITDA margin of 17.9% and record EBIT margin of 16.8%, up 30 bps YoY
- EPS increased 1% to SEK 3.85 in Q4
- Full-year 2025 sales of SEK 152,409M with organic growth of 3%
- Full-year adjusted operating income of SEK 24,664M, margin of 16.2%
- Board proposed dividend of SEK 6.40/share, up from SEK 5.90 prior year
- Share price surged following the results

## Analysis
- ASSA ABLOY's ability to achieve record margins despite a challenging residential market and FX headwinds demonstrates pricing power and operational discipline. The 4% organic growth in Q4 indicates strong momentum in commercial access solutions and electromechanical products. The dividend increase signals management confidence in continued cash generation.

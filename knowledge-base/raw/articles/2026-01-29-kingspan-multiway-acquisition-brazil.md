# Kingspan Data Solutions Acquires Brazil's Multiway to Expand Data Center Presence

## Key Details
- **Transaction Date**: January 22, 2026 (announced January 29, 2026)
- **Target**: Multiway Industria de Infraestrutura Ltda. (Brazil)
- **Founded**: 1998
- **Products**: Mission-critical data center components including containment systems, racks, structural ceilings, cable management, enclosures, cable trays
- **Market Position**: Strong presence in complex data center projects across Brazil
- **Division**: Joins Kingspan Data Solutions
- **Strategic Rationale**: Adds local manufacturing capability and specialist expertise for Latin America's fast-growing digital infrastructure market
- **Significance**: First investment in Data Solutions segment in Brazil

## Analysis
- The Multiway acquisition extends Kingspan's data center infrastructure business into Latin America, one of the fastest-growing regions for data center construction
- This bolt-on deal is consistent with Kingspan's strategy of building a global data center solutions platform through the Advances division
- Brazil's data center market is growing rapidly as hyperscale cloud providers expand Latin American infrastructure
- The acquisition adds local manufacturing capability, which is increasingly important as data center customers demand shorter lead times and regional supply chain resilience

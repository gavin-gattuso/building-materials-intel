# Bain 2026 M&A Report: Building Products Shifts from Scale to Scope

## Deal Activity (2025 YTD through Sept 30)
- North America: +33% deal value
- EMEA: -48% deal value
- Asia-Pacific: -44% deal value

## Major Transactions
- Lowe's acquired Foundation Building Materials: $8.8B (pro customer reach)
- Home Depot acquired GMS (pro channel expansion)
- CRH acquired Eco Material Technologies: $2.1B (fly ash/pozzolans)
- Holcim bid for Xella (complementary building materials)

## Key Insights
- Shift from scale plays to scope/capability acquisitions
- Top 6 cement producers control 65-70% of US capacity (highly consolidated)
- Sanitary/plumbing fragmented (top 5 hold ~25% European share)
- Frequent acquirers: 9.6% TSR vs 2.7% for inactive companies

## Recommendation
- Due diligence shifting from cost synergies to revenue synergies
- Distinguish temporary cyclical weakness from structural deficiencies

# Interfor Consensus: Moderate Buy with 47% Upside Potential; Analysts Split

## Key Details
- Consensus rating: Moderate Buy (3 Buy, 1 Hold, 1 Sell)
- Average price target: CAD $12.98-$13.94 (depending on source)
- Price target range: CAD $9.84 - CAD $16.80
- Implied upside: ~47% from current trading levels
- Raymond James analyst Daryl Swetlishoff favors Canfor and Interfor over West Fraser
- Analysts see geographic diversification strategy as key differentiator

## Analysis
- The 47% implied upside to consensus targets is the highest among the Canadian lumber producers, suggesting Interfor may offer the best risk/reward for a lumber recovery trade.
- The Sell rating amidst a generally bullish consensus indicates at least one analyst sees structural rather than cyclical challenges for Interfor.
- Raymond James' preference for Canfor and Interfor over West Fraser likely reflects the smaller companies' greater operating leverage to a lumber price recovery.
- At current depressed prices, all three Canadian lumber producers are being valued primarily on balance sheet and asset value rather than near-term earnings.

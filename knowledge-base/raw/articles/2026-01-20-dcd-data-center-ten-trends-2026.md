# Data Centers: The Ten Main Trends for 2026

## Key Points
- Ten key trends identified for data center industry in 2026
- AI-related demand continues to drive massive construction investment
- Europe witnessing emergence of mega-campus data centers exceeding hundreds of megawatts
- First tangible gigawatt campuses in Europe expected within next 12 months
- Industry must consider what tools and construction techniques meet demand alongside high-density needs
- Power constraints and sustainability targets remain critical challenges
- Decarbonization focus driving interest in low-carbon concrete and sustainable steel for construction
- Construction timelines and material availability are key bottlenecks for industry growth
